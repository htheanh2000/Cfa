Theory of the firm · two views

Shareholder vs Stakeholder primacy.

Hai triết lý quản trị doanh nghiệp đối lập. CFA L1 yêu cầu hiểu cả hai và biết khi nào áp dụng. Modern thinking nghiêng về balanced stakeholder approach.

Shareholder primacy
Goal
Maximize shareholder wealth — Friedman doctrine 1970
Logic
Shareholders là residual claimant → bear most risk → đáng được ưu tiên
Decision
NPV positive cho equity holders = approve
Phổ biến
US, UK · common-law jurisdictions
Stakeholder primacy
Goal
Balance lợi ích của tất cả stakeholders — employees, customers, community
Logic
Long-term firm value đến từ sustainable relationships, không chỉ equity returns
Decision
Cân nhắc impact lên multiple parties
Phổ biến
EU, Japan · civil-law & co-determination systems

§ Convergence — Business Roundtable 2019 statement signed by 181 US CEOs reframed corporate purpose từ "shareholders only" sang "all stakeholders". ESG investing accelerate xu hướng này.

Mendelow matrix · power × interest

Mapping power × interest.

Manager phân loại stakeholder theo 2 trục: power (ability to affect firm) và interest (mức độ quan tâm). 4 ô → 4 strategies.

  1. High power · High interest — Manage closely (key players) Major shareholders, large lenders, board, regulators. Engage liên tục, consult trước khi quyết định lớn. VD: BlackRock, sovereign wealth fund.
  2. High power · Low interest — Keep satisfied Government agencies, large institutional creditors. Không quan tâm hằng ngày nhưng có thể veto. Báo cáo định kỳ, tránh "đánh thức gấu".
  3. Low power · High interest — Keep informed Employees, retail shareholders, community groups, customers. Voice mạnh nhưng formal power thấp → newsletters, town halls, AGM.
  4. Low power · Low interest — Monitor (minimal effort) General public, distant suppliers. Chỉ track xem có shift sang ô khác không. Tiết kiệm engagement budget.

Primary stakeholders · 6 groups

The six core groups.

01
Shareholders · equity owners
Residual claim. Voting rights · dividends · capital appreciation. Bear nhiều rủi ro nhất nhưng cũng có upside không giới hạn.
02
Debtholders · creditors / bondholders
Fixed claim — interest + principal. Không vote thường xuyên nhưng có covenants. Risk-averse: muốn firm ổn định, không lấy rủi ro.
03
Employees
Wages + benefits + job security. Human capital invested → firm-specific. Unions tăng bargaining power. Concern: layoffs, safety.
04
Customers
Quality, price, after-sales support, data privacy. Power = switching cost + concentration. B2B customer thường có nhiều power hơn B2C.
05
Suppliers
Timely payment, fair terms, long-term partnership. Critical input supplier (vd TSMC) có power rất lớn — gần như equity-like influence.
06
Government & community
Tax compliance, regulation, environment, jobs. Power = regulatory + license to operate. ESG framework thường focus ở đây.

§ Hierarchy claims — Trong bankruptcy: secured debt → unsecured debt → preferred equity → common equity. Shareholders cuối cùng nên CFA L1 gọi họ là "residual claimant".

Activist investors · 3 archetypes

Who are the activists?

Activist investor = shareholder mua significant stake để pressure management thay đổi strategy, capital allocation hoặc governance. CFA L1 phân biệt 3 loại theo motivation và horizon.

  1. Activist hedge funds — aggressive, short-to-medium horizon Carl Icahn, Pershing Square (Bill Ackman), Elliott Management. Mua 5-10% stake, đòi spin-off / buyback / new CEO. Goal: unlock value nhanh, exit trong 1-3 năm.
  2. Pension funds — long-horizon, governance-focused CalPERS, CalSTRS, Norway GPFG. Hold rất lâu (decades) → quan tâm long-term governance, board independence, executive comp, ESG. Ít confrontational hơn hedge fund.
  3. Sovereign wealth funds — strategic, often passive Norway GPFG (~$1.6T), GIC Singapore, ADIA, Temasek. Stake lớn nhưng thường passive, focus diversification. Một số (Temasek) active hơn ở local market.

§ Index funds — Vanguard, BlackRock, State Street ("Big Three") cộng lại nắm ~20% S&P 500. Technically "passive" nhưng voting power khổng lồ → de facto governance influence.

Activist playbook · escalation ladder

The tactics.

01
Schedule 13D filing
SEC filing bắt buộc khi vượt 5% ownership với "active" intent (vs 13G passive). Trong 10 ngày phải disclose. Stock thường jump khi 13D xuất hiện.
02
Public letters & presentations
Activist viết open letter chỉ trích management, đăng website (vd Pershing Square 100-slide deck). Mục đích: rally other shareholders + media pressure.
03
Board seat demands
Đòi nominate 1-3 directors. Có thể negotiate quietly hoặc thông qua proxy fight. Board seat = ongoing influence on strategy.
04
Proxy fight — escalation cuối
Activist thuyết phục shareholders vote bầu slate riêng vào board. Tốn kém ($10-50M legal/PR). Chỉ dùng khi negotiation thất bại.

§ Escalation order — Thường: behind-scenes letter → public 13D → public campaign → board seat negotiation → proxy fight → litigation. Mỗi bước cost & conflict tăng dần.

Activist screening · which firms get hit

When activists target a company.

§ Pattern · activist target profile Activists thường target firm có (1) underperforming stock so với peers, (2) cash-rich balance sheet chưa được trả về shareholders, (3) entrenched management hoặc weak board, (4) sum-of-parts > market cap (conglomerate discount), hoặc (5) governance flaws như poison pills, classified board, dual-class shares. Firm "fat & lazy" với balance sheet conservative là magnet — activist tin có thể unlock value bằng buybacks, divestitures, hoặc operational improvements.
§ Defensive plays · how firms resist Poison pill (shareholder rights plan): nếu một bên vượt threshold (vd 15%), other shareholders được mua thêm shares discount → dilute hostile bidder. Staggered board: chỉ 1/3 directors lên ballot mỗi năm → activist mất 2-3 năm để control. Supermajority voting: yêu cầu 2/3 hoặc 80% approval cho mergers. White knight: tìm friendly acquirer.

§ Empirics — Bebchuk et al. (2015) tìm thấy hedge fund activism associated with 5-year improvement in operating performance. Nhưng cũng debate: short-termism vs long-term value creation.

Common shareholder · core rights

What shareholders get.

  1. Voting rights — bầu directors, vote major decisions Standard: 1-share-1-vote. Vote cho board, M&A, charter amendments, executive comp (say-on-pay). Proxy = uỷ quyền vote nếu không dự AGM.
  2. Dividend rights — share trong lợi nhuận distribute Không phải obligatory — board quyết định declare dividend. Common shareholder nhận sau khi pref shareholder + creditor đã được trả.
  3. Residual claim — tài sản còn lại khi liquidate Sau khi trả hết debt và preferred. Trong bankruptcy thường common = $0 (wiped out). Đây là source of upside & downside asymmetry.
  4. Preemptive rights — quyền mua first khi firm phát hành cổ mới Bảo vệ chống dilution. Existing shareholder được mua pro-rata trước khi shares chào public. Common ở EU, ít hơn ở US.
  5. Information rights — annual report, 10-K, 10-Q Yêu cầu disclosure đầy đủ. Inspect books trong some jurisdictions. Class action right nếu management breach fiduciary duty.

Voting structure · two models

1-share-1-vote vs Dual-class.

Cấu trúc voting quyết định ai control firm. CFA L1 yêu cầu hiểu trade-off: simplicity & alignment vs founder protection & long-term vision.

1-share-1-vote
Mỗi share
Có đúng 1 vote. Economic interest = voting interest.
Pros
Alignment hoàn hảo · simple · "one share, one vote, one value"
Cons
Founder mất control khi dilute · vulnerable to short-term activist
Examples
Đa số S&P 500 firms, hầu hết EU listed
Dual-class shares
Cấu trúc
Class A (public) = 1 vote · Class B (founders) = 10 votes hoặc 20
Pros
Founder giữ vision long-term · không bị activist ép short-termism
Cons
Minority shareholders ít power · governance risk · MSCI/FTSE giảm weight
Examples
Alphabet (A=1, B=10, C=0), Meta, Snap (Class A=0!), Berkshire

§ Sunset clauses — Một số IPO mới (vd Lyft, Palantir) phải có "sunset" — dual-class tự convert sang 1-1-1 sau 7-15 năm. Index providers (S&P) đã exclude một số firms không có sunset.

Board election · two methods

Cumulative vs Statutory voting.

Statutory voting
Rule
1 vote per share, per each director seat separately
Effect
Majority shareholder wins tất cả seats
Minority
Khó có representation — 49% shareholder không bầu được ai
Phổ biến
Default ở nhiều US states, simpler
Cumulative voting
Rule
Shareholder có (shares × seats) votes, có thể concentrate hết vào 1 candidate
Effect
Minority có thể giành ít nhất 1 seat nếu đủ shares
Formula
Min shares cần = (S × N)/(D+1) + 1, S=total, N=seats wanted, D=board size
Phổ biến
Bảo vệ minority shareholder · phổ biến tại Vietnam (Luật Doanh nghiệp 2020)
§ Cumulative voting · worked example
Board 5 seats, total 1,000,000 shares → tổng votes = 5,000,000. Minority có 200,000 shares = 1,000,000 votes.
Trong statutory: minority có 200K votes/seat → 5 lần thua majority 800K. Trong cumulative: minority dồn hết 1M votes vào 1 candidate → có thể elect 1 director (cần > 1M/(5+1) ≈ 167K, đã có 1M).

Bondholder protection · debt indenture

Bondholder protection covenants.

Bondholders không có voting rights nhưng có contractual protection trong debt indenture. Vi phạm covenant = technical default → có thể accelerate debt.

§ Affirmative (positive) covenants — issuer PHẢI làm Pay interest & principal đúng hạn · Maintain insurance trên major assets · File audited financial statements định kỳ · Maintain corporate existence (không dissolve) · Comply with laws · Maintain certain financial ratios (vd interest coverage > 3x, debt/EBITDA < 4x).
§ Negative (restrictive) covenants — issuer KHÔNG được làm Limit on additional debt (no more than X total leverage) · Restrict dividends & buybacks (giữ cash để pay creditor) · Limit asset sales (giữ collateral pool) · Limit M&A activity · No subsidiary guarantees ngoài approved list · Negative pledge (không pledge tài sản cho creditor khác trừ khi pari passu).

§ Seniority & security — Senior secured > senior unsecured > subordinated > mezzanine. Secured có specific collateral (real estate, equipment). Trong bankruptcy, thứ tự này quyết định ai được trả trước → impact yield demand.

Agency cost · conflict #1

Shareholders vs Bondholders.

Shareholders muốn upside (residual + unlimited). Bondholders muốn safety (capped upside, sợ downside). Conflict gốc: shareholders thích risk hơn bondholders.

  1. Asset substitution — sau khi vay, switch sang risky projects Firm vay với rate priced cho safe projects, rồi đầu tư vào high-risk. Upside về shareholders; downside chia với bondholders. Covenants hạn chế asset use.
  2. Excessive dividends & buybacks — strip cash khỏi firm Pay out cash cho shareholders → giảm asset coverage cho bondholders. Restrictive covenants limit payout ratio hoặc require minimum retained earnings.
  3. Underinvestment / debt overhang — Myers (1977) Khi firm distressed, shareholders không muốn invest vào positive-NPV project nếu benefit chủ yếu về bondholders. → Reject good projects, value destroyed.
  4. Additional borrowing — dilute existing bondholder claim Issue thêm pari-passu hoặc senior debt → existing bondholder bị diluted. Negative pledge + debt incurrence covenants chặn lại.

Agency cost · conflict #2

Controlling vs Minority shareholders.

Family firms, dual-class, EM markets — controlling shareholder có thể extract private benefits of control on minority's expense. Common ở Asia, LatAm, Europe.

  1. Tunneling — chuyển tài sản sang related-party entity Controlling shareholder bán assets cho own private firm dưới giá thị trường, hoặc buy supplies từ related party trên giá. Minority shareholder mất giá trị.
  2. Excessive executive compensation — nepotism Hire family members ở C-suite với pay package không justify. Minority pay phần share của mình cho lương "fluffy" này.
  3. Suboptimal acquisitions — empire-building Mua công ty khác để control nhiều hơn (signaling, prestige) thay vì tạo value. Destroy NPV của firm nhưng controlling shareholder vẫn có private benefits.
  4. Mitigations — minority protections Cumulative voting · independent directors · related-party transaction disclosure · "majority of minority" approval cho RPT · class action lawsuits · regulator oversight.

Agency cost · conflict #3 · classic

Manager vs Owner — agency cost of equity.

Jensen & Meckling (1976). Khi ownership và control tách biệt, manager có incentive khác với shareholder. Đây là foundational problem của corporate governance.

Manager incentives
Goal
Job security · prestige · perks · empire-building
Risk
Risk-averse — diversifying acquisitions để protect career, không tối ưu cho shareholder
Horizon
Short — focus EPS quarterly để bonus, ít invest R&D long-term
Cash
Hoard cash để autonomous, "free cash flow problem" (Jensen)
Owner (shareholder) interests
Goal
Maximize firm value & total shareholder return
Risk
OK với positive-NPV risky projects vì có diversified portfolio
Horizon
Long — willing to invest R&D, growth
Cash
Distribute excess cash via dividends/buybacks if no NPV+ projects

§ Mitigation tools — Equity comp (stock options, RSUs) align manager với shareholder · Independent board oversight · Activist investors + market for corporate control (hostile takeover threat) · Auditor + regulator.

ESG · stakeholder engagement

ESG & stakeholder engagement.

ESG framework systematize stakeholder concerns thành 3 pillars. Investors ngày càng integrate vào valuation: $30T+ AUM globally apply ESG criteria (GSIA 2022).

  1. E — Environmental Climate change exposure, carbon emissions (Scope 1/2/3), water use, biodiversity, waste, supply chain footprint. Material cho energy, industrials, materials sectors.
  2. S — Social Employee safety & turnover, diversity & inclusion, customer privacy & data security, product safety, community impact, human rights in supply chain.
  3. G — Governance Board independence, executive comp alignment, audit quality, anti-corruption, shareholder rights, voting structure, related-party transactions. Most material across all sectors.
  4. Engagement methods Direct dialogue · proxy voting · shareholder resolutions · public letters · divestment threat · collaborative engagement (Climate Action 100+, PRI). Voice + Exit theo Hirschman.

Practice · Question 01 · Original

Cumulative voting.

§ Vignette
A company has 1,000,000 shares outstanding and is electing 5 directors at the upcoming AGM under cumulative voting. A minority investor owns 200,000 shares. Which statement is most accurate?
  1. AThe minority investor cannot elect any director because she lacks a majority stake.
  2. BThe minority investor can guarantee the election of at least one director by concentrating all 1,000,000 votes on one candidate.
  3. CCumulative voting and statutory voting produce the same outcome when the same shareholders vote.
CFA Level I Corporate · 05.07 — Investors & Stakeholders
05.07 — Stakeholders 20 / 30
Hint · Question 01

Spot the keywords.

§ Keywords Total 1,000,000 shares, 5 directors, cumulative voting, minority owns 200,000. Threshold formula: (S × N)/(D+1) + 1.
  1. Hint 1 Cumulative voting: tổng votes của minority = shares × seats = 200,000 × 5 = 1,000,000 votes. Có thể dồn hết vào 1 candidate.
  2. Hint 2 Threshold để guarantee 1 seat: (1,000,000 × 1)/(5+1) + 1 ≈ 166,668 shares. Minority có 200,000 > threshold → đủ điều kiện.
  3. Hint 3 A sai vì cumulative khác statutory. C sai — statutory winner-takes-all, cumulative cho phép minority có seat → kết quả khác nhau khi same votes cast.

Dịch đề.

§ Vignette
Một công ty có 1,000,000 cổ phiếu outstanding và đang bầu 5 directors tại AGM theo cumulative voting. Một minority investor sở hữu 200,000 cổ phiếu. Phát biểu nào chính xác nhất?
  1. AMinority investor không thể bầu được director nào vì không có majority stake.
  2. BMinority investor có thể đảm bảo bầu được ít nhất một director bằng cách dồn 1,000,000 votes vào một candidate.
  3. CCumulative voting và statutory voting cho kết quả giống nhau khi cùng shareholders vote.
CFA Level I Corporate · 05.07 — Investors & Stakeholders
05.07 — Stakeholders 22 / 30
Answer · Question 01

Answer: B — Minority can elect 1.

✓ Correct — B
Cumulative voting cho mỗi shareholder shares × seats votes, có thể concentrate. Minority: 200,000 × 5 = 1,000,000 votes. Threshold cho 1 seat: (1,000,000 × 1)/(5+1) + 1 ≈ 166,668 shares. Minority có 200,000 > 166,668 → đủ shares; concentrate 1M votes vào 1 candidate là guarantees 1 seat. Đây là raison d'être của cumulative voting — bảo vệ minority.
✗ Wrong — A
Sai vì confuse cumulative với statutory. Trong statutory, minority 20% sẽ thua 80% ở mỗi seat. Nhưng đề nói cumulative — designed exactly to enable minority representation. Reading the regime carefully là key.
✗ Wrong — C
Sai. Statutory: winner-takes-all all seats. Cumulative: minority có thể giành ít nhất một số seats proportional. Same votes cast nhưng allocation rule khác → outcomes khác nhau hoàn toàn.
Key takeaway
Cumulative voting threshold: shares cần > (Total × Seats wanted)/(Total seats + 1). Quy tắc đơn giản: để giành k seats trên D-board cần > k/(D+1) tổng shares. Đây là protection cốt lõi cho minority shareholders.

Activist tactics.

§ Vignette
An activist hedge fund just acquired a 6% stake in a US-listed industrial company and intends to push for a strategic review and a board refresh. Management has rejected initial private engagement. Which sequence of escalation is most consistent with standard activist practice?
  1. AFile Schedule 13G → litigate immediately → run a proxy fight.
  2. BFile Schedule 13D → publish open letter and presentation → negotiate board seats → run proxy fight if rejected.
  3. CQuietly accumulate to 20% without filing → demand a private settlement → never go public.

Hint · Question 02

Spot the keywords.

§ Keywords Stake 6% > 5% threshold, active intent (board change), management rejected private engagement. Standard escalation: 13D → public → board → proxy.
  1. Hint 1 Vượt 5% với active intent → bắt buộc 13D, không phải 13G (passive). 13G chỉ cho long-only passive holders. → A loại.

05.07 — Stakeholders 26 / 30
Answer · Question 02

Answer: B — Standard escalation.

✓ Correct — B
Standard activist playbook: (1) 13D bắt buộc khi vượt 5% với active intent — disclose trong 10 ngày. (2) Public letter / presentation rally other shareholders + media (Pershing Square style). (3) Negotiate board seats — hợp tác nếu management cooperate. (4) Proxy fight chỉ khi negotiation thất bại — đắt và risky. Order phản ánh nguyên tắc: cost & conflict tăng dần, dùng escalation cuối cùng khi cần.
✗ Wrong — A
13G chỉ cho passive holders (vd index fund không có active intent). Activist với strategic review intent phải file 13D. Ngoài ra, "litigate immediately" là last resort, không phải first move — quá đắt, alienate shareholders.
✗ Wrong — C
Accumulate > 5% không filing = vi phạm SEC rules (13D 10-day disclosure). "Never go public" cũng kém hiệu quả — public pressure là weapon chính của activist. Quiet accumulation chỉ work khi dưới 5%.
Key takeaway
Activist escalation ladder: 13D → public letter → board seat negotiation → proxy fight → litigation. 13D vs 13G: D = active intent (5%+10 days), G = passive (long-only filers, longer deadline). Memorize đúng order — common exam trap.

Practice · Question 03 · Original

Agency conflict.

§ Vignette
A highly leveraged firm with restrictive debt covenants is considering two mutually exclusive projects of equal NPV: Project A with low risk and stable cash flows, and Project B with high risk, high upside but a meaningful default scenario. Management is purely shareholder-aligned and there are no covenants restricting investment policy. Which choice is most consistent with the shareholder–bondholder agency conflict?
  1. AChoose Project A — bondholders prefer stable cash flows; shareholders are indifferent given equal NPV.
  2. BChoose Project B — shareholders capture the upside while bondholders absorb part of the downside (asset substitution).
  3. CReject both — shareholders prefer cash distribution to any new investment when the firm is leveraged.
CFA Level I Corporate · 05.07 — Investors & Stakeholders
05.07 — Stakeholders 28 / 30
Hint · Question 03

Spot the keywords.

§ Keywords Highly leveraged, equal NPV, A=low risk, B=high risk + default scenario, management shareholder-aligned, no investment covenants. Concept: asset substitution.
  1. Hint 1 Equity = call option trên firm value (Merton). Shareholders thích volatility cao vì upside unlimited, downside limited ở $0.
  2. Hint 2 Bondholders bear part of downside (default scenario của B = bondholder loss). Shareholders share upside không chia với bondholders. → Wealth transfer từ BH sang SH.

Answer · Question 03

Answer: B — Asset substitution.

✓ Correct — B
Asset substitution (Jensen-Meckling 1976): equity là call option trên firm value — shareholders thích volatility cao vì capped downside (limited liability) nhưng unlimited upside. Khi firm leveraged và no investment covenants, shareholder-aligned management chọn B để transfer wealth từ bondholders sang shareholders. Bondholders priced debt cho safer profile của firm; switching sang risky project = ex-post wealth grab. Đây là raison d'être của investment policy covenants.
✗ Wrong — A
Sai vì shareholders không indifferent giữa A và B dù equal NPV. NPV bằng nhau ở total firm level, nhưng distribution giữa SH và BH khác — B chuyển value từ BH sang SH. Câu hỏi yêu cầu identify conflict, không phải optimal social outcome.
✗ Wrong — C
"Reject both" mô tả debt overhang / underinvestment (Myers 1977) — relevant khi firm severely distressed VÀ benefit chủ yếu cho bondholders. Đây không phải scenario đề cho. Equal NPV positive + leveraged firm → asset substitution incentive, không phải underinvestment.
Key takeaway
Three SH-BH agency conflicts to memorize: (1) Asset substitution — switch sang risky projects; (2) Excessive payout — strip cash via dividends/buybacks; (3) Debt overhang / underinvestment — reject good projects khi distressed. Mỗi conflict được countered bằng một loại covenant cụ thể.
