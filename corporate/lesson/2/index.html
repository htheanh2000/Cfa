Governance · concept

What is corporate governance?

§ CFA Definition Corporate governance là hệ thống internal controls and procedures dùng để quản lý corporations, nhằm (1) xác định rights và responsibilities của stakeholders, (2) đảm bảo accountability của management, và (3) giải quyết conflicts of interest giữa các bên.
  1. Mục đích — align management với shareholder interests Vì owners (shareholders) không điều hành trực tiếp, cần hệ thống đảm bảo managers act vì lợi ích của owners chứ không vì lợi ích cá nhân.
  2. Phạm vi — bảo vệ ALL stakeholders, không chỉ shareholders Modern governance mở rộng xem xét creditors, employees, customers, community → stakeholder theory (vs. shareholder primacy).
  3. Cơ chế — internal (board, bylaws) + external (law, market) Internal: board oversight, internal audit, compensation design. External: regulations, takeover market, credit rating agencies.

Stakeholders · 6 groups

Who the stakeholders are.

Mỗi stakeholder có interests khác nhau, và nhiều khi conflict với nhau. Good governance balance các interests này.

01
Shareholders
Owners · muốn max share price & dividend · residual claim · long-term value creation.
02
Creditors / bondholders
Lenders · muốn được trả interest & principal · risk-averse · conflict với shareholders về risk-taking.
03
Managers & employees
Muốn compensation cao, job security, career growth. Có thể pursue empire-building thay vì profit max.
04
Board of directors
Elected by shareholders. Oversee management. Fiduciary duty to shareholders và all stakeholders.
05
Customers & suppliers
Customers: quality + fair price. Suppliers: on-time payment + stable orders. Commercial relationships.
06
Government & community
Tax compliance, job creation, environmental impact. Regulations, licensing, social license to operate.

Core conflict

The principal-agent problem.

§ The conflict Principal (shareholders) hire agent (managers) để điều hành firm. Nhưng agent có information advantage (asymmetric info) và incentive khác principal — dẫn đến managers có thể act vì mình, không vì owners.
Manager vs Shareholder
Risk
Mgmt risk-averse (job at stake), shareholder diversified → want more risk-taking
Horizon
Mgmt short-term (bonus cycle), shareholder long-term
Perks
Private jets, lavish offices — benefit mgmt, cost shareholder
Empire
Mgmt pursue growth/M&A kể cả value-destroying — bigger firm, bigger pay
Shareholder vs Creditor
Risk
Shareholder upside unlimited → prefer high-risk projects. Creditor fixed upside → risk-averse
Dividend
Shareholder muốn dividend cao → creditor sợ asset stripping
Leverage
Shareholder muốn tăng debt (tax shield); creditor sợ default risk tăng
Control
Creditor dùng covenants để ràng buộc behavior của firm

Solutions · mitigation

Aligning incentives.

Governance design các công cụ để giảm agency cost — làm managers "think like owners".

  1. Equity-based compensation — stock options, RSU Manager sở hữu shares → tự động align với shareholders. Nhưng coi chừng short-termism và earnings manipulation.
  2. Performance-linked pay — bonus tied to KPIs Pay tied to ROE, EPS growth, TSR. Long-term incentive plans (LTIP) reward multi-year performance, giảm short-term gaming.
  3. Independent board oversight Majority independent directors có thể fire CEO, set compensation, approve strategy. Key check trên executive power.
  4. Debt covenants & monitoring Creditors bảo vệ mình qua covenants: debt/EBITDA limits, dividend restrictions, CAPEX caps.
  5. Market for corporate control — hostile takeovers Nếu mgmt perform kém, share price giảm → raiders mua và thay mgmt. Disciplines weak management externally.

Board · roles

The board.

R1
Hire & fire CEO
Board select CEO, evaluate annually, replace nếu underperform. Succession planning là fiduciary duty.
R2
Approve strategy
Review và approve long-term strategy, major M&A, CAPEX lớn, dividend policy, capital structure decisions.
R3
Set compensation
Approve executive pay, incentive plans. Compensation committee thường require independent directors.
R4
Oversee risk & audit
Ensure internal controls, financial reporting integrity, compliance với regulations. Audit committee key.

§ Board structure — One-tier (US, UK): một board gồm executive + non-executive. Two-tier (DE, NL): supervisory board (oversight) tách khỏi management board (execute). Cả hai aim cho check & balance.

Committees · 3 key

The three committees.

Board delegate specialized work cho committees. Listed company rules (NYSE, SGX) thường require majority independent directors trong committees.

Audit
Role
Oversee financial reporting, internal audit, external auditor
Members
100% independent, ≥1 financial expert
Focus
Integrity of financials, control weaknesses, fraud risk
Compensation
Role
Design và approve executive pay, incentive plans
Members
Majority/all independent — avoid conflict
Focus
Pay for performance, benchmark, say-on-pay vote
§ Nominating / Governance Committee Identify & recommend directors mới, review board composition, board evaluation, và succession planning cho CEO và key executives. Đảm bảo board có diverse skills, independence, và expertise.

Shareholder · tools

How shareholders exercise voice.

  1. Annual General Meeting (AGM) Yearly forum shareholders bầu directors, approve dividends, ratify auditor, và chất vấn management. Bắt buộc theo luật hầu hết jurisdictions.
  2. Proxy voting — vote remotely Shareholder không dự AGM có thể đưa quyền vote qua proxy statement. Proxy advisors (ISS, Glass Lewis) tư vấn vote cho institutional investors.
  3. Say-on-pay — advisory vote on executive comp Shareholders vote (thường advisory) trên executive compensation package. Fail say-on-pay = red flag về governance.
  4. Shareholder proposals & activism Shareholder có stake đủ (thường 1-3%) có thể đưa proposal lên AGM. Activist investors (Icahn, Elliott) push strategic changes.
  5. Litigation — derivative lawsuits Shareholder kiện directors/officers vì vi phạm fiduciary duty. Last-resort mechanism, mạnh nhưng tốn kém và chậm.

Voting · mechanics

Voting structures.

Statutory voting
Rule
1 share = 1 vote cho mỗi director seat
Effect
Majority shareholder win TẤT CẢ seats — minority khó có đại diện
Example
Holder 51% → elect 100% board seats
Cumulative voting
Rule
Votes = shares × seats · có thể dồn cho 1 candidate
Effect
Protect minority — với đủ votes tập trung có thể elect ít nhất 1 director
Example
Holder 20% với 5 seats → concentrate → likely win 1 seat

§ Dual-class impact — Khi có dual-class (founder shares 10 votes/share), ngay cả cumulative voting cũng không giúp minority. Google/Meta: founders < 20% economics nhưng > 50% voting power.

Risk · red flags

Governance red flags.

  1. Weak or captured board Few independent directors, CEO cũng là Chairman, directors với long tenure, hoặc gắn chặt với management — mất oversight.
  2. Poor disclosure & transparency Delayed filings, unusual accounting, frequent auditor changes, material weaknesses in internal controls.
  3. Related-party transactions Giao dịch giữa company và insiders/family/sister companies với non-arm's-length terms. Value extraction rủi ro. Common ở emerging markets.
  4. Excessive executive compensation Pay không tương xứng với performance, golden parachutes lớn, repricing options sau price giảm → misaligned incentives.
  5. Anti-takeover defenses Poison pills, staggered boards, supermajority requirements — entrench management, giảm market discipline, thường giảm shareholder value.

Practice · Question 01 · Original

Agency scenario.

§ Vignette
The CEO of a mature firm pushes a large debt-funded acquisition in a new industry. The deal would triple firm size, increasing the CEO's compensation (tied to total assets) but adding substantial integration risk. Shareholders worry the deal will destroy value. Which principal-agent problem does this BEST illustrate?
  1. ARisk aversion — CEO avoids risky projects to protect her job.
  2. BEmpire-building — CEO pursues size over value to increase personal compensation.
  3. CShort-termism — CEO prioritizes quarterly earnings over long-term strategy.
CFA Level I CORPORATE · 05.02 — Q1 ORIGINAL
Corporate · 05.02 15 / 26
Hint · Question 01

Spot the keywords.

§ Keywords Triple firm size. Compensation tied to total assets. New industry (unrelated). Destroy value.
  1. Hint 1 "Tripling size" + "pay tied to assets" = pay incentive link đến size, không phải value. Classic signal of empire-building.
  2. Hint 2 Risk aversion sẽ khiến CEO tránh big deals. Ở đây CEO push deal rủi ro cao → ngược với risk aversion. Loại A.
  3. Hint 3 Short-termism focus quarterly earnings. Large M&A thường hit short-term earnings (integration cost) → không phù hợp short-termism. Loại C.

Practice · Question 01 · Tiếng Việt

Dịch đề.

§ Vignette
CEO của một firm mature đẩy mạnh một thương vụ M&A lớn tài trợ bằng nợ vào ngành mới. Deal sẽ gấp ba quy mô firm, tăng compensation CEO (gắn với total assets) nhưng có risk integration lớn. Shareholders lo sợ deal sẽ phá hủy giá trị. Vấn đề principal-agent nào BEST mô tả tình huống này?
  1. ARisk aversion — CEO né projects rủi ro để giữ việc.
  2. BEmpire-building — CEO theo đuổi quy mô thay vì giá trị để tăng compensation cá nhân.
  3. CShort-termism — CEO ưu tiên quarterly earnings hơn long-term strategy.
CFA Level I CORPORATE · 05.02 — Q1 VIỆT
Corporate · 05.02 17 / 26
Answer · Question 01

Answer: B — Empire-building.

✓ Correct — B
Empire-building là classic principal-agent problem nơi manager pursue firm size/growth kể cả khi value-destroying, vì personal comp tỷ lệ với size. Ở đây: (1) comp tied to total assets → size-based. (2) Triple size + unrelated industry = value-destroying risk. (3) CEO cá nhân benefit, shareholders bear risk. Textbook empire-building.
✗ Wrong — A
Risk aversion = manager avoid risky projects. CEO ở đây đang push deal rủi ro cao → ngược với risk aversion. Risk aversion thường dẫn đến under-investment, không over-investment.
✗ Wrong — C
Short-termism = prioritize near-term earnings hơn long-term value. Large debt-funded M&A thường hurt near-term earnings (interest, integration cost) → không phải short-termism. Scenario này là long-term size expansion.
Key takeaway
Fix empire-building: pay tied to value (TSR, ROIC), không tied to size (assets, revenue). Independent board phải scrutinize M&A để ngăn value-destroying deals. CFA test hay dùng "compensation linked to assets/revenue" như signal của empire-building.

Board composition.

§ Vignette
An analyst reviews a listed company's board: CEO also serves as Chairman (combined role). The 9-person board has 3 independent directors, and the audit committee includes the CFO as a member. Which of the following is the most significant governance concern?
  1. ACombined CEO/Chairman role concentrates power and weakens oversight.
  2. BOnly 3 of 9 directors are independent — below majority threshold.
  3. CThe CFO sits on the audit committee — violates independence of audit oversight.

§ Keywords CFO on audit committee. CEO = Chairman. Only 3/9 independent. Which is most significant?

Dịch đề.

§ Vignette
Một analyst xem board của công ty listed: CEO kiêm Chairman (combined role). Board 9 người có 3 independent directors, và audit committee bao gồm CFO làm thành viên. Đâu là lo ngại governance nghiêm trọng nhất?
  1. ACEO kiêm Chairman tập trung quyền lực và làm yếu oversight.
  2. BChỉ 3/9 directors là independent — dưới ngưỡng đa số.
  3. CCFO ngồi trong audit committee — vi phạm independence của audit oversight.
CFA Level I CORPORATE · 05.02 — Q2 VIỆT
Corporate · 05.02 21 / 26
Answer · Question 02

Answer: C — CFO in audit committee.

✓ Correct — C
Audit committee MUST be 100% independent theo best practice (NYSE, SGX, HKEx listing rules). CFO là executive — người chịu oversight — nên không thể ngồi trên committee oversee chính mình. Đây là bright-line rule violation, không phải matter of degree. Audit committee phê duyệt external auditor, review financial statements — CFO participating là fundamental conflict of interest.
✗ Wrong — A
Combined CEO/Chairman role là concern thật — concentrates power, giảm oversight. NHƯNG nhiều jurisdictions (US đặc biệt) vẫn permit. Mitigation qua lead independent director. Less severe than bright-line rule violation trong option C.
✗ Wrong — B
Independent majority (>50%) là best practice nhưng NOT uniformly required toàn cầu. 3/9 dưới majority là red flag. Tuy nhiên, vẫn là matter of degree — một số firms có <50% independent vẫn compliant với local rules. CFO trong audit committee là direct violation.
Key takeaway
Committee independence hierarchy: audit committee = 100% independent (strictest). Compensation = majority/all (strict). Nominating = majority (medium). Khi CFA hỏi "most significant" governance concern, tìm bright-line rule violation trước, sau đó đến best-practice deviations.

ESG integration approach.

§ Vignette
A portfolio manager builds a strategy that excludes all companies involved in tobacco, weapons manufacturing, and fossil fuel extraction, regardless of their valuation or financial performance. Which ESG integration approach does this represent?
  1. ANegative screening — exclude specific sectors on values/ethics basis.
  2. BBest-in-class — select highest ESG performers within each sector.
  3. CActive ownership — engage with companies to improve ESG outcomes.
CFA Level I CORPORATE · 05.02 — Q3 ORIGINAL
Corporate · 05.02 23 / 26
Hint · Question 03

Spot the keywords.

§ Keywords Excludes tobacco, weapons, fossil fuel. Regardless of valuation. → Approach?
  1. Hint 1 "Excludes all companies in [sectors]" = định nghĩa textbook của negative screening (exclusionary screening).
  2. Hint 2 "Regardless of valuation" cho thấy decision rule dựa trên values/ethics, không phải financial analysis. Classic negative screen.
  3. Hint 3 Best-in-class SELECT trong sector. Active ownership ENGAGE và vote. Negative screen EXCLUDE. Phân biệt bằng verb.

Practice · Question 03 · Tiếng Việt

Dịch đề.

§ Vignette
Một portfolio manager xây dựng strategy loại trừ tất cả công ty liên quan đến thuốc lá, sản xuất vũ khí, và khai thác nhiên liệu hóa thạch, bất kể valuation hay financial performance. Đây là cách tiếp cận ESG integration nào?
  1. ANegative screening — loại trừ các sector cụ thể dựa trên values/ethics.
  2. BBest-in-class — chọn các ESG performer tốt nhất trong từng sector.
  3. CActive ownership — engage với companies để cải thiện ESG outcomes.
CFA Level I CORPORATE · 05.02 — Q3 VIỆT
Corporate · 05.02 25 / 26
Answer · Question 03

Answer: A — Negative screening.

✓ Correct — A
Negative (exclusionary) screening loại trừ companies/sectors dựa trên values hoặc ethics. Scenario fits perfectly: (1) exclude entire sectors (tobacco, weapons, fossil fuel) — sector-based exclusion. (2) "regardless of valuation" — decision không dựa trên financial metrics. (3) Values-based reasoning. Đây là form lâu đời nhất của ESG investing, bắt đầu từ các religious funds thế kỷ 18.
✗ Wrong — B
Best-in-class SELECT highest ESG scorers TRONG mỗi sector — không exclude sector hoàn toàn. Ví dụ: giữ oil & gas nhưng chọn company có carbon footprint thấp nhất. Scenario exclude cả sector → không phải best-in-class.
✗ Wrong — C
Active ownership = HOLD shares và engage với management/vote proxies để improve ESG. Scenario không mention engagement — chỉ mention exclusion ở portfolio construction. Active ownership và negative screen có thể complement nhau nhưng distinct approaches.
Key takeaway
ESG approaches phân biệt bằng action: Negative screen = EXCLUDE. Positive/Best-in-class = SELECT top. Thematic = TARGET theme (green, water). Active ownership = ENGAGE + VOTE. Impact = MEASURE outcomes. CFA hay test bằng cách mô tả action → match với approach.
