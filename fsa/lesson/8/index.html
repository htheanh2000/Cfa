Long-term debt · foundation

Bond issuance.

Khi firm phát hành bond, issue price phụ thuộc vào tương quan giữa coupon rate (cố định trong indenture) và market yield tại ngày phát hành. Ba kịch bản cơ bản:

§ Issue price = PV of cash flows discounted at market yield
Issue Price = Σ Coupon / (1+y)t + Face / (1+y)n
Coupon = market yield → bond at par. Coupon > market yield → bond at premium (issue price > face). Coupon < market yield → bond at discount (issue price < face).

§ Tại issuance, BS ghi Bond payable = issue price (KHÔNG phải face value). Premium/discount được embed trong carrying value và amortize dần qua effective interest method đến đáo hạn → carrying value hội tụ về face value.

Bond mechanics

Premium vs Discount.

Premium bond
Điều kiện
Coupon rate > market yield tại issuance
Issue price
Issue price > face value (e.g. $1,050 vs $1,000 face)
Carrying value
Bắt đầu cao hơn face → giảm dần về face khi đáo hạn
Interest expense
Coupon paid > interest expense → premium amortize giảm liability mỗi kỳ
Discount bond
Điều kiện
Coupon rate < market yield tại issuance
Issue price
Issue price < face value (e.g. $950 vs $1,000 face)
Carrying value
Bắt đầu thấp hơn face → tăng dần về face khi đáo hạn
Interest expense
Interest expense > coupon paid → discount amortize tăng liability mỗi kỳ

§ Cả hai trường hợp, total interest expense over life = total coupons paid ± premium/discount amortization. Premium giảm interest expense tổng; discount tăng.

Amortization · the rule

Effective interest method.

Cả IFRS lẫn US GAAP đều yêu cầu effective interest method cho bond amortization. Straight-line chỉ allowed dưới US GAAP nếu kết quả không materially different.

§ Effective interest rule Interest expenset = Carrying valuet-1 × Market yield at issuance. Coupon paid là cash outflow cố định. Difference giữa interest expense và coupon paid = premium/discount amortization, cộng/trừ vào carrying value.
  1. Step 1 — Tính interest expense Interest expense = beginning carrying value × market yield (locked tại issuance, không thay đổi khi market rate biến động sau đó).
  2. Step 2 — Tính amortization Premium: amortization = coupon − interest expense (giảm carrying value). Discount: amortization = interest expense − coupon (tăng carrying value).
  3. Step 3 — Update carrying value Premium: CVt = CVt-1 − amortization. Discount: CVt = CVt-1 + amortization. Tại maturity, CV = face value.

Bond carrying value

Bond carrying value over life.

§ Carrying value evolution
CVt = CVt-1 + Interest Expenset − Coupont
Interest Expenset = CVt-1 × y0
y0 = market yield at issuance (effective rate). Premium bond → CV ↓ over time (interest exp < coupon). Discount bond → CV ↑ over time (interest exp > coupon). Par bond → CV constant = face.

§ Important — carrying value KHÔNG bằng market value của bond sau issuance. Market value reflect biến động interest rate; carrying value chỉ amortize theo y0. Analyst đôi khi adjust BS bằng fair value của debt khi compute leverage ratios.

Cash flow impact — Coupon payment cash outflow chia làm 2 phần trên CFS: interest portion (CFO under US GAAP, có thể CFO/CFF under IFRS) + principal at maturity (CFF). Issuance proceeds là CFF inflow.

End of life

Bond derecognition.

Bond có thể được derecognize tại maturity (paid in full) hoặc early extinguishment — firm mua lại bond từ market hoặc call option. Early extinguishment tạo ra gain/loss trên IS.

§ Gain/loss on early extinguishment
Gain (Loss) = Carrying Value − Repurchase Price
Carrying value > repurchase price → gain (firm trả ít hơn book debt). Carrying value < repurchase price → loss. Gain/loss ghi nhận trên Income Statement, thường ở "other income/expense" hoặc separate line nếu material.
  1. Common trigger — interest rate giảm Khi market rate giảm sau issuance, market price bond tăng → repurchase tốn nhiều hơn → loss. Ngược lại, rate tăng → repurchase rẻ → gain.
  2. Disclosure — footnote bắt buộc Firm phải disclose nature, amount, và lý do của early retirement. Analyst quan tâm vì nó signal về interest rate strategy hoặc capital restructuring.
  3. Cash flow classification Repurchase price ghi nhận đầy đủ là CFF outflow. Gain/loss là non-cash adjustment cho CFO (indirect method).

Lease accounting · post-reform

When to capitalize a lease.

IFRS 16 (2019) và ASC 842 (2019) reform định nghĩa lease accounting. Hầu hết operating lease cũ (off-BS) nay phải capitalize → right-of-use asset + lease liability.

§ IFRS 16 · single lessee model Mọi lease có term > 12 months và underlying asset không low-value đều capitalize. Lessee ghi nhận: (1) Right-of-use asset (asset side) + (2) Lease liability = PV of lease payments. KHÔNG còn distinction operating vs finance dưới IFRS.
  1. Initial recognition ROU asset = PV(lease payments) + initial direct costs + estimated dismantling. Lease liability = PV(lease payments) discounted at implicit rate hoặc lessee incremental borrowing rate.
  2. Subsequent measurement ROU asset depreciate (thường straight-line). Lease liability amortize qua effective interest method. Total expense front-loaded vs operating cũ.
  3. IS impact 2 lines: Depreciation (operating) + Interest expense (financing/operating). EBITDA, EBIT tăng vs operating lease cũ; interest expense tăng → coverage ratios xấu hơn.

US GAAP · ASC 842 dual model

Finance vs Operating lease (lessee).

US GAAP giữ dual model. Cả 2 loại đều capitalize on BS, nhưng IS & CFS treatment khác nhau.

Finance lease
Bản chất
Substantively transfer ownership/control — tương đương mua tài sản bằng debt
IS expense
2 lines: Depreciation (asset) + Interest expense (liability) — front-loaded
CFS
Interest portion: CFO. Principal portion: CFF
Triggers
Title transfer, bargain purchase option, term > 75% useful life, PV ≥ 90% fair value, specialized asset
Operating lease
Bản chất
Pure rental — lessee không "own" risk & rewards
IS expense
1 line: Lease expense straight-line over term
CFS
Toàn bộ payment: CFO outflow
Triggers
KHÔNG meet bất kỳ trigger finance lease nào → default operating

§ Cả 2 loại đều có ROU asset + lease liability on BS dưới ASC 842. Khác biệt nằm ở IS và CFS classification, ảnh hưởng EBITDA, coverage ratios, và operating CF metrics.

Lessor side

Lessor classification — 3 types.

SLT
Sales-type lease
Manufacturer/dealer dùng lease như sales channel. Recognize gross profit upfront (sales revenue − COGS) + interest income over term. Asset removed khỏi BS lessor.
DFL
Direct-financing lease
Lessor là financial intermediary, không có dealer profit. KHÔNG recognize gross profit upfront — chỉ interest income over term. Asset thay bằng net investment in lease.
OPL
Operating lease
Asset ở lại BS lessor, depreciate normally. Recognize lease income straight-line. Tương tự rental business.
⊕
Triggers for sales-type / DFL
Tương tự finance lease cho lessee: transfer ownership, bargain purchase option, term ≥ 75% useful life, PV ≥ 90% fair value, specialized asset.

§ IFRS 16 kept dual model on lessor side: finance lease vs operating lease. Sales-type và direct-financing là phân khúc của finance lease dưới US GAAP.

Post-employment benefits

Defined Benefit vs Defined Contribution.

Defined Contribution (DC)
Risk bearer
Employee chịu investment risk & longevity risk
Employer obligation
Limited to contribution amount trong kỳ — sau khi pay, hết nghĩa vụ
BS impact
Không có long-term liability. Chỉ accrued contribution payable nếu chưa pay
IS expense
Pension expense = contribution của kỳ. Đơn giản, predictable
Ví dụ
401(k), Vietnamese voluntary pension fund
Defined Benefit (DB)
Risk bearer
Employer chịu investment + longevity + actuarial risk
Employer obligation
Promise specific future benefit (e.g. % salary × years of service)
BS impact
Net pension liability/asset = PBO − fair value plan assets
IS expense
Multi-component: service cost, interest cost, expected return on assets, amortization
Ví dụ
Traditional corporate pension, public sector schemes

§ Trend toàn cầu: shift từ DB → DC vì DB tạo huge balance sheet liabilities và earnings volatility từ actuarial assumptions.

DB pension components

Pension expense components.

§ DB periodic pension cost (US GAAP)
Pension Expense = SC + IC − ER ± Amort
SC Service cost — PV of benefits earned trong kỳ hiện tại (operating). IC Interest cost — PBO × discount rate (non-operating). ER Expected return on plan assets (non-operating, smoothed). Amort Amortization of past service cost & actuarial G/L.
§ IFRS vs US GAAP — actuarial G/L treatment IFRS (IAS 19) — service cost (P&L) + net interest on net pension liability (P&L) + remeasurements (OCI, no recycling). US GAAP — full expense aggregated on IS, actuarial G/L initially in OCI then amortized to IS qua corridor method.

§ Total periodic pension cost (TPPC) = change in net pension liability + employer contributions = expense (IS) + remeasurement (OCI). Analyst dùng TPPC để compare across firms vì nó capture full economic cost regardless of where it's reported.

Shareholders' equity

Equity components.

CS
Share capital (Common Stock)
Par value × shares issued. Par chỉ là nominal accounting figure (thường $0.01). Một số jurisdiction dùng "no-par stock" — toàn bộ proceeds vào common stock account.
APIC
Additional Paid-in Capital
Issue price − par value, × shares. Nếu IPO $20/share, par $0.01 → APIC = $19.99/share. Cũng include premium từ stock option exercise và conversion features.
RE
Retained Earnings
Cumulative net income − cumulative dividends − cumulative treasury stock retired. Internal funding source quan trọng nhất; reflect lifetime profitability.
TS
Treasury Stock (contra)
Buyback shares ghi tại cost, trừ khỏi total equity. Buyback giảm shares outstanding → tăng EPS, signal undervaluation. KHÔNG tạo gain/loss khi tái phát hành.
AOCI
AOCI
Accumulated Other Comprehensive Income — cumulative OCI items: FX translation, unrealized gains/losses AFS/FVOCI, pension remeasurements, cash flow hedges.
NCI
Non-controlling Interest
Equity của minority shareholders trong subsidiaries consolidated. Trên consolidated BS, ghi tách riêng dưới equity (post-2009 reform).

Equity-linked compensation

Stock-based compensation.

Cả IFRS (IFRS 2) và US GAAP (ASC 718) đều require expense recognition over vesting period dựa trên fair value tại grant date. Compensation expense hit IS, contra entry vào equity (APIC).

  1. Stock options — Black-Scholes valuation Fair value tại grant date (Black-Scholes hoặc binomial model) → expense ratably over vesting period (e.g. 4 năm). Khi exercise, nhân viên trả strike price → cash inflow + APIC tăng.
  2. RSUs (Restricted Stock Units) Fair value = stock price tại grant date × units. Đơn giản hơn options. Expense over vesting period. Khi vest, shares phát hành (hoặc cash equivalent), APIC tăng.
  3. ESOPs (Employee Stock Ownership Plans) Trust mua shares cho employees (often leveraged). Compensation expense = contribution to ESOP trust mỗi kỳ. Diluted EPS tính included shares.
  4. Dilution effect — analyst adjustment Diluted EPS dùng treasury stock method (options) hoặc if-converted (convertibles). Heavy SBC firms (tech) nên adjust EPS bằng cách add back SBC vào CFO khi compute "core" cash earnings.

§ CFS treatment — SBC là non-cash expense → add back vào Net Income trong CFO (indirect method). Khi options exercise, cash từ employee ghi CFF inflow; tax benefit từ exercise được reform 2017 chuyển vào CFO (US GAAP).

Analyst lens

Why this matters.

  1. Bond carrying value ≠ market value Khi rate biến động, fair value debt lệch carrying value. Adjust BS toward fair value để có true economic leverage trước khi compute D/E, D/A.
  2. Lease capitalization changed everything Pre-2019, off-BS operating lease che giấu leverage. Post-IFRS 16/ASC 842, nhiều firms (retailers, airlines) thấy debt jump 30-100% on BS. Cross-period comparison cần adjust.
  3. DB pensions — hidden risk Underfunded pension = effectively long-term debt. Discount rate assumption sensitive: giảm 1% có thể tăng PBO 10-20%. Always check footnote actuarial assumptions.
  4. SBC dilution — equity story Tech firms thường có SBC = 10-20% revenue. Excluded từ "adjusted EBITDA" but is real shareholder cost. Watch dilution trend trong shares outstanding.

Bond carrying value.

§ Vignette

  1. A$915,750 — carrying value remains constant until maturity.
  2. B$930,895 — carrying value increases as the discount amortizes.
  3. C$901,500 — carrying value decreases as coupon exceeds interest expense.

Spot the keywords.

§ Keywords Discount bond (coupon 4% < yield 6%). Effective interest method. End of Year 1 carrying value.

Answer: B — $930,895.

✓ Correct — B

Answer: B — Cash ↓ $25M, Equity ↓ $25M.

✓ Correct — B
Buyback 1M × $25 = $25M. Asset side: Cash ↓ $25M. Equity side: Treasury stock (contra-equity) +$25M → Total equity ↓ $25M. Treasury stock ghi tại cost method (most common). A = L + E vẫn balance. CFS: $25M outflow trong CFF.

Recap

What to remember.

  1. Bond carrying value evolves toward face Premium → CV ↓; discount → CV ↑. Effective interest = beginning CV × original yield. Early extinguishment: gain/loss = CV − repurchase price.
  2. IFRS 16 single model vs ASC 842 dual IFRS: mọi lease > 12mo capitalize, 2 expense lines. US GAAP: finance vs operating, cả 2 đều on BS, expense pattern khác.
  3. DB pension expense = SC + IC − ER ± Amort IFRS đẩy actuarial G/L vào OCI no recycling. US GAAP qua corridor amortization. TPPC capture full economic cost.
  4. Equity = CS + APIC + RE + AOCI − Treasury Treasury stock = contra-equity tại cost, không gain/loss. SBC qua APIC, expense over vesting. Watch dilution.
