Foundation · before forecast

Why analyze past & present first?

Forecast (m07) chỉ đáng tin nếu base = thực tế đã hiểu kỹ. Past & present analysis answer 3 câu cơ bản: (1) công ty làm gì để kiếm tiền? (2) kết quả historical thế nào — trends, drivers, anomalies? (3) earnings có "real" không hay accounting-driven?

α
Forecast = past × judgment
Bất kỳ revenue/margin forecast nào đều start từ historical base. Skip past analysis = forecast trên cát.
β
Spot accounting from operations
Past analysis reveal accounting choices (depreciation method, revenue recognition timing, accrual reserves) đã inflate hoặc smooth earnings. Forecast cần adjust away.
γ
Identify anomalies — one-offs vs recurring
Last year's big gain từ asset sale ≠ recurring earning. Need to clean historical earnings để forecast trên "core" base.
δ
Management track record
Past capital allocation (M&A win/lose, buyback timing, dividend consistency) reveal management skill — quan trọng cho forward thesis.

Workflow

A 3-step framework.

  1. 1 · Business model — what + how Hiểu products/services, customers, geography, delivery. Bao nhiêu segments? Where does revenue come from? Pricing power level? B2B vs B2C? Recurring vs one-off? Output: 1-page business map.
  2. 2 · Historical financials — trends + drivers 5–10 năm: revenue growth (organic vs M&A), margin trends, profitability ratios (ROIC, ROE, ROA), capital structure history, cash flow profile. Output: spreadsheet với common-size statements + trend chart.
  3. 3 · Quality assessment — earnings + management Quality of earnings (accruals, non-recurring items, accounting choices). Capital allocation track record. Governance & insider behavior. Output: red flag list + adjusted "core earnings" base cho forecast.

§ Sequence matters — không jump vào ratios trước khi hiểu business. Nếu không hiểu what company sells/to-whom, ratios chỉ là digits không context.

Step 1 · business model

Dissect the business model.

B1
Products & services
Liệt kê SKU/service categories. Identify flagship products (Pareto: ~20% products thường đem ~80% revenue/profit).
B2
Customer concentration
Top 10 customer % revenue. High concentration = key risk (supplier loss = revenue cliff). Geography concentration cũng vậy.
B3
Pricing power
Price-taker (commodity) vs price-maker (brand). Test: company có raise price during inflation mà không lose volume không?
B4
Revenue recurrence
Subscription/contract = recurring (predictable). Project-based / transactional = lumpy. SaaS, utilities = recurring; construction, M&A advisory = lumpy.
B5
Distribution channel
Direct (own salesforce, own retail) vs indirect (wholesaler, distributor, marketplace). Direct = higher margin nhưng heavier capex; indirect = lower margin nhưng scale faster.
B6
Operating model
Asset-heavy (manufacturer, utility) vs asset-light (consulting, software). Drives capital intensity, ROIC, working capital cycle.

Step 2 · revenue + cost

Revenue + cost structure.

Revenue dissection
Decomposition
Revenue = Volume × Price. Track Volume growth (units sold, customers, transactions) vs Price growth (ASP, mix shift, geography premium) separately.
Organic vs M&A
Organic growth (same-store, like-for-like) phản ánh business strength thật. M&A growth là one-time trừ khi chiến lược roll-up bền vững.
Segment view
Most companies report 2-5 segments. Fast-growing segment có thể bị che bởi declining segment ở consolidated number.
Geography view
FX impact: report constant-currency growth vs reported. EM exposure = beta cao; DM exposure = stable.
Cost structure
Fixed vs variable
High fixed cost = operating leverage (small revenue change → big margin change, both ways). Airlines, hotels, factories = high op leverage.
COGS analysis
Major input commodities + supplier power. Vd hãng F&B: track sugar, wheat, packaging cost. Tech: track salary inflation cho engineers.
SG&A
Selling cost (sales team, marketing) — phụ thuộc growth strategy. G&A — fixed overhead, scale theo size firm. Track SG&A % revenue trends.
R&D
R&D % revenue: tech ~15-25%, pharma ~15-20%, manufacturer ~3-5%. Capitalized vs expensed (US GAAP expense; IFRS allows capitalize). Affect comparability.

Margins + profitability ratios

Margin trends & profitability ratios.

§ Margin ladder
Gross margin = (Revenue − COGS) / Revenue
Operating margin = EBIT / Revenue (loại tax + interest)
Net margin = Net Income / Revenue
EBITDA margin = EBITDA / Revenue (proxy cho operating cash flow)
Gross margin trend → product mix & input cost story. Operating margin → operational efficiency. Net margin → financing & tax overhead. Track 5-10Y trend; compare với peer median.
§ Profitability ratios
ROA = NI / Avg Total Assets
ROE = NI / Avg Common Equity
ROIC = NOPAT / Invested Capital = EBIT(1−t) / (Debt + Equity − Cash)
ROIC là metric "purest" cho operational profitability — strip out capital structure choice. ROIC vs WACC: nếu ROIC > WACC liên tục, công ty đang tạo value; ROIC < WACC = phá value bất kể growth.

DuPont decomposition

DuPont — decompose ROE.

DuPont separate ROE thành 3 (hoặc 5) drivers — phân biệt margin growth, asset efficiency, leverage. Identify nguồn ROE growth là healthy (margin/turnover) hay risky (leverage).

§ 3-step DuPont
ROE = Net margin × Asset turnover × Equity multiplier
ROE = (NI/Rev) × (Rev/Avg Assets) × (Avg Assets/Avg Equity)
Net margin = profitability per dollar of sales. Asset turnover = sales per dollar of assets (efficiency). Equity multiplier = financial leverage. ROE ↑ chỉ vì equity multiplier ↑ = leveraged growth → fragile.
§ 5-step (extended) DuPont
ROE = (NI/EBT) × (EBT/EBIT) × (EBIT/Rev) × (Rev/Avg Assets) × (Avg Assets/Avg Equity)
Tách Tax burden, Interest burden, Operating margin, Asset turnover, Leverage. Tách interest burden cho phép xem liệu interest expense có eat margin growth không. Useful cho heavy-debt company (REIT, utility, leveraged buyouts).

Cash flow quality

Earnings vs cash.

"Profit là opinion; cash là fact." Reported earnings có thể méo do accounting choices; cash flow khó méo hơn (nhưng vẫn có). Quality check: operating cash flow consistently > net income = high quality.

§ Quality ratio Cash earnings ratio = CFO / Net Income. ≥ 1 = high quality (cash backs reported profit). < 1 sustained = red flag (earnings driven by accruals — receivables ↑, deferred revenue ↓, working capital build).
§ Free Cash Flow (FCF) tiers FCFF = CFO + Int(1−t) − Capex (cash to all capital providers).
FCFE = CFO − Capex + Net Borrowing (cash to equity holders).
Track FCF margin = FCF / Revenue và FCF conversion = FCF / Net Income. Mature company: FCF conversion ≥ 70%.
§ Red flags Reported earnings tăng nhanh nhưng CFO flat hoặc giảm. Receivables tăng nhanh hơn revenue (collection issue, channel stuffing). Inventory tăng nhanh hơn COGS (slow-moving stock, demand drop). Cap-ex chronic > depreciation (treadmill — phải invest mãi để giữ tốc độ).

Capital allocation history

How management spent the cash.

Cash generated → 5 destinations: reinvest in business (capex), M&A, debt repay, dividend, buyback. Decade-long pattern reveal management priorities và competence.

C1
Reinvestment (capex + R&D)
High capex/depreciation ratio = growing capacity. ≈ 1 = maintenance only. Track ROIC on incremental capex — nếu giảm theo time, marginal returns suy yếu.
C2
M&A
Track record win/lose. Goodwill impairment = past M&A failed (acquired assets không deliver). Roll-up strategy có thể successful (constellation: Berkshire, Constellation Software) hay disaster (HP-Autonomy, Yahoo-Tumblr).
C3
Buybacks
Buyback ở undervalued price = value creation per share. Buyback ở overvalued (peak) = value destruction. Track price-paid vs intrinsic value estimate.
C4
Dividends
Track payout ratio + dividend growth + coverage ratio. Cut dividend = signal financial stress hoặc shift strategy. "Dividend aristocrat" (25+ năm increase) = signal stable cash + management discipline.

Working capital cycle

Cash conversion cycle.

§ Cash Conversion Cycle (CCC)
CCC = DSO + DIO − DPO
DSO = (Receivables / Revenue) × 365 — days collect customer
DIO = (Inventory / COGS) × 365 — days hold inventory
DPO = (Payables / COGS) × 365 — days defer payment supplier
CCC ngắn = ít vốn lưu động, hiệu quả. Walmart, Amazon CCC âm (collect khách trước khi trả supplier) — supplier financed growth. CCC dài = vốn bị tied up trong inventory + receivables, cần funding.
§ Trends to spot DSO ↑ sustained = collection issues hoặc bigger but slower-paying customers. DIO ↑ sustained = slow-moving inventory, demand softening, có thể write-down sắp đến. DPO ↑ sustained = stretching supplier (có thể signal cash crunch hoặc successful negotiation).

Step 3 · quality + management

Quality of earnings + management.

Quality of earnings
Accruals
High accruals (NI >> CFO) = potential earnings management. Track accruals ratio = (NI − CFO) / Avg Total Assets.
One-off items
Asset sale gains, restructuring charges, FX gains, litigation settlements. Strip away cho "core earnings" base.
Accounting choices
Depreciation method (straight-line vs accelerated), inventory (FIFO vs LIFO), revenue recognition timing, capitalize vs expense R&D.
Disclosure changes
Mid-year change in segment reporting, restated prior year — usually cover something. Read footnote carefully.
Management track record
Capital allocation skill
Past M&A ROIC. Buyback timing vs price. Dividend consistency.
Guidance accuracy
Track vs guided revenue/EPS. Repeated overshooting → conservative guidance (good). Repeated missing → optimistic guidance (concern).
Insider behavior
Insider buying ở deep value times = positive signal. Insider selling clustered ở peak = caution. Stock options vesting timing có thể explain.
Compensation alignment
Comp tied to long-term ROIC/TSR vs short-term EPS. Stock-heavy comp aligns; cash-heavy = potentially short-term focus.

Synthesis

The company snapshot.

§ Snapshot template Business: 1 sentence "what + how + who". "Sells X to Y customers via Z channel".
Revenue: Last 5Y CAGR + organic vs M&A split + segment mix.
Margin: Gross/Operating/Net trend + peer-comparison band.
Returns: ROIC trend + ROIC vs WACC verdict.
Cash: CFO/NI ratio + FCF margin + capex intensity.
Capital allocation: Top 2 use of cash last 5Y + assessment.
Quality: Top 3 quality red flags (or "clean").
Management: CEO tenure + capital allocation track record + comp alignment.

§ Snapshot phải 1 trang. Nếu cần 5 trang để summarize, bạn chưa simplify. Hard discipline: nếu không thể explain past performance trong 1 trang thì chưa thực sự understand.

Practice · Question 01 · Original

DuPont driver.

§ Vignette
Saigon Industrial Co. reports the following 3-year ROE trend: 12% (Year 1) → 16% (Year 2) → 20% (Year 3). Over the same period: net margin held flat at 8%, asset turnover declined from 1.5× to 1.3×, and the equity multiplier rose from 1.0× to 1.92×. Which of the following best describes the source of the ROE improvement?
  1. AOperational excellence — improving net margin and rising asset turnover both indicate stronger underlying business performance.
  2. BIncreased financial leverage — the equity multiplier nearly doubled while operating drivers (margin, turnover) deteriorated or held flat. ROE growth is leveraged, not earned.
  3. CImproved asset efficiency — asset turnover rose and was the primary driver of the higher ROE.

Hint · Question 01

Spot the driver.

§ Keywords NM flat · Asset turnover ↓ · Equity multiplier ↑↑. ROE = NM × AT × EM. Verify từng driver.
  1. Hint 1 Year 1: 8% × 1.5 × 1.0 = 12% ✓. Year 3: 8% × 1.3 × 1.92 = ~20% ✓. ROE tăng 67% (12 → 20).

  3. Hint 3 EM = Assets/Equity. EM tăng = Debt-to-Equity tăng. ROE growth chỉ vì leverage = không sustainable + tăng risk → fragile.

  3. CAsset efficiency cải thiện — asset turnover tăng và là driver chính của ROE cao hơn.

Answer · Question 01

Answer: B — leverage.

✓ Correct — B
DuPont 3-step: ROE = NM × AT × EM. Verify: Year 1 = 8% × 1.5 × 1.0 = 12% ✓; Year 3 = 8% × 1.3 × 1.92 = 19.97% ≈ 20% ✓. Decompose source: NM flat (0% contribution to ROE growth); AT giảm 1.5 → 1.3 = −13% (negative drag); EM tăng 1.0 → 1.92 = +92% (sole driver). ROE tăng 67% chỉ vì leverage tăng — không phải vì business mạnh hơn. Đây là fragile ROE: nếu rate ↑ hoặc downturn, leveraged firm chịu interest expense + default risk cao hơn.
✗ Wrong — A
Sai cốt lõi — net margin giữ nguyên 8% (không cải thiện), asset turnover giảm (1.5 → 1.3, không tăng). Đáp án A đảo ngược fact.
✗ Wrong — C
Sai phần "asset turnover tăng" — turnover thực tế giảm. Asset turnover đóng góp negative tới ROE growth, không phải driver.
Key takeaway
DuPont reveal nguồn gốc ROE growth — đặc biệt important khi compare các năm. ROE growth từ margin/turnover = healthy operational improvement. ROE growth từ leverage = financial engineering, tăng fragility. Khi đọc proxy CEO khoe ROE 20% — verify driver trước khi praise.

Earnings quality red flag.

§ Vignette
Hanoi Tech reports the following 3-year metrics: revenue growth 25% / 30% / 28% per year; net income growth 35% / 40% / 45%; cash from operations growth 5% / 3% / −2%; days sales outstanding (DSO) rose from 45 days to 95 days. Which is most likely indicated?
  1. AHigh-quality growth — net income is outpacing revenue, indicating expanding margins and operational excellence.
  2. BEarnings quality concerns — net income growth far exceeds CFO growth and DSO has more than doubled, suggesting reported earnings are accrual-driven rather than cash-backed; collection issues or potential channel-stuffing should be investigated.
  3. CStrong working capital management — rising DSO indicates the company is offering attractive credit terms to win market share, which is supported by accelerating revenue.

Answer · Question 02

Answer: B — quality concerns.

✓ Correct — B
Cardinal red flag: NI growth diverges from CFO growth. NI tăng 35-45%/năm; CFO chỉ tăng 5%, 3%, thậm chí giảm 2%. Divergence sustained = earnings được drive bởi accruals, không phải cash. Receivables tăng (DSO 45 → 95 ngày = gần gấp đôi) là confirm: revenue được book nhưng tiền chưa thu. Khả năng: (a) collection deteriorating (customer financial stress), (b) channel-stuffing (ship distributor cuối quý để hit revenue target, distributor sẽ return), (c) aggressive revenue recognition (ghi nhận trước thực tế delivery). Cần investigate footnotes về revenue recognition policy + customer concentration + bad debt provision.
✗ Wrong — A
Sai — NI vượt revenue có thể do margin mở rộng (good) HOẶC accrual-driven (bad). DSO tăng gấp đôi + CFO không follow = clearly bad scenario, không phải good. Margin expansion thật sẽ đi kèm cash conversion mạnh, không phải tách rời.
✗ Wrong — C
Tự lừa logic — "DSO tăng = good credit terms để win market" có thể đúng trong 1 năm. Nhưng khi DSO tăng gấp đôi mà CFO giảm = không phải growth strategy, là cash crunch warning. "Revenue gia tốc" cũng có thể là book revenue mà không deliver.
Key takeaway
"NI grows, CFO doesn't" = quality red flag #1. Combine với DSO tăng rapid = channel-stuffing hoặc collection collapse. Investigate: revenue recognition policy, top customer concentration, bad debt provision adequacy. Adjust historical earnings về cash basis trước khi forecast.

Practice · Question 03 · Original

Buyback assessment.

§ Vignette
Vietnam Consumer Goods Co. has executed three large share buyback programs over the past five years, all at prices substantially above analyst-estimated intrinsic value. Two years after the most recent buyback, the stock has declined 40% and the company has now taken a write-down on previously repurchased treasury stock value. Which is the most accurate conclusion about management's capital allocation decisions?
  1. ABuybacks are always accretive to shareholders because they reduce share count, increase EPS, and signal management confidence — the price decline is unrelated to the buyback decisions.
  2. BThe buybacks destroyed shareholder value because management repurchased shares above intrinsic value. Effective buyback strategy requires repurchasing below intrinsic value; otherwise, capital is being transferred from continuing shareholders to exiting ones.
  3. CThe buybacks were value-neutral because shareholders who sold received cash equal to their proportional ownership; only the subsequent stock decline affected returns, not the buyback itself.

Hint · Question 03

Spot value vs price.

§ Keywords Buyback at price above intrinsic value + subsequent decline 40%. Buyback có luôn accretive không?
  1. Hint 1 Buyback = company mua lại stock của exiting shareholders. Nếu price > intrinsic value → company over-pay → wealth transfer từ continuing shareholders → exiting shareholders.
  2. Hint 2 Effective buyback: mua khi stock undervalued. Buffett rule: "buy back stock only when significantly below intrinsic value, otherwise dividend or hold cash".
  3. Hint 3 EPS accretion từ buyback chỉ là accounting illusion (giảm shares outstanding). Per-share intrinsic value chỉ tăng nếu buy below intrinsic — không phải bất kỳ giá nào.

Answer · Question 03

Answer: B — value destruction.

✓ Correct — B
Buyback effectiveness phụ thuộc 100% vào price paid vs intrinsic value. Khi management buy back ở price > intrinsic value: (a) company over-pay cash; (b) wealth transfers từ continuing shareholders (intrinsic share của họ shrink) sang exiting shareholders (nhận giá premium); (c) per-share intrinsic value của continuing shareholders giảm dù share count giảm. Subsequent stock decline + treasury stock write-down confirm management mua peak. EPS accretion là kế toán illusion: net income chia ít shares = EPS cao, nhưng underlying business value per share thực sự giảm. Buffett principle: "buy back only significantly below intrinsic value; otherwise dividend or retain".
✗ Wrong — A
Sai cốt lõi — "buybacks always accretive" là common myth. Buyback above intrinsic value destroys per-share value bất chấp EPS tăng. EPS accretion ≠ value creation. Stock decline 40% + write-down chính là evidence buyback decision sai.
✗ Wrong — C
Sai logic — "value-neutral" only true nếu buyback at exactly intrinsic value. Above intrinsic = wealth transfer. Treasury stock write-down explicitly recognize accounting loss của over-paid buyback — không "value neutral" chút nào.
Key takeaway
Buyback là tool, không phải always-good. Effective khi: (a) price < intrinsic value, (b) management có conviction về intrinsic value estimate, (c) alternative use of cash (capex, M&A, debt) có lower IRR. Track record buyback ở peak = capital allocation red flag, ảnh hưởng forecast assumption về future capital deployment skill.
