Reading the Handbook

Required vs Recommended.

Mỗi sub-standard trong CFA Standards Handbook chia 2 lớp: Required conduct ("must" — vi phạm là vi phạm Standard) và Recommended procedures ("should" — best practice firm). Phân biệt rõ vì exam thường bẫy thí sinh chọn "violation" cho recommended-only failure.

Required — must
Wording
"Members must…", "shall not…", "may not…"
Apply to
Member & candidate cá nhân — cấm hoặc bắt buộc làm
Vi phạm
Trigger PCP investigation. Sanction range: cautionary letter → revocation
Ví dụ
I(B): "must use reasonable care to maintain independence and objectivity"
Recommended — should
Wording
"Members should…", "Firms should consider…", "It is recommended…"
Apply to
Firm-level procedures + individual best practice
Vi phạm
KHÔNG tự động vi phạm Standard. Nhưng absence of recommended procedure có thể được considered tăng nặng nếu Required violated
Ví dụ
VI(B): "Firms should establish blackout periods, pre-clearance, and reporting requirements"

Std I · Professionalism (1/2)

I(A) Knowledge of Law · I(B) Independence.

I(A) Knowledge of Law
Required
Hiểu & tuân laws/regulations applicable + Code & Standards. Khi conflict: tuân theo cái strict hơn. Không knowingly participate hoặc assist vi phạm. Phải dissociate khỏi vi phạm — báo supervisor + nếu không sửa thì resign.
Recommended
Stay current laws/regs (CE). Review compliance policy annually. Maintain reference current laws.
Common pitfall
Member không có duty whistleblow bên ngoài employer (CFA Standards không bắt buộc) — nhưng có quyền và một số jurisdiction luật yêu cầu.
I(B) Independence & Objectivity
Required
Reasonable care + judgment giữ independence. Không offer/solicit/accept gift/benefit/compensation compromise judgment.
Modest gift OK
Giá trị nhỏ (token, business meal on-site, ticket sự kiện thường niên giá thị trường) — không violate. Lavish trip / cash / lodging upgrade = vi phạm.
Recommended
Firm restricted/watch list. Pre-approval cho gift > threshold. Independent research budget cho sell-side analyst (không phụ thuộc issuer).

Std I · Professionalism (2/2)

I(C) Misrepresentation · I(D) Misconduct.

I(C) Misrepresentation
Required
Không knowingly misrepresent investment analysis, recommendations, qualifications, services. Plagiarism = vi phạm — phải attribute ideas, charts, words. Quoting consensus economic data từ Bloomberg/Reuters thường OK without attribution.
Forms
Misstating credentials, omitting material info, guaranteeing future performance, misrep services capability, copying research mà không attribute.
Recommended
Written sources policy. Templates cho disclosures. Training cho marketing material review.
I(D) Misconduct
Required
Không engage in conduct involving dishonesty, fraud, deceit; hoặc commit acts adversely reflect professional reputation, integrity, competence.
Scope
Cover both professional và personal conduct nếu reflect on professional reputation. Vd DUI, domestic violence, fraud cá nhân — có thể trigger.
Limit
Personal political, religious, social activity KHÔNG vi phạm trừ khi adversely reflect competence/integrity.

§ Lưu ý — I(D) cover cả off-the-job conduct nếu it bears on professional reputation. Đây là Standard rất broad — case-by-case judgment.

Std II · Integrity of Capital Markets

II(A) MNPI · II(B) Manipulation.

II(A) Material Nonpublic Information
Required
Possess MNPI → không trade, không cause others trade, không recommend. Phải encourage issuer disclose broadly.
Material test
Reasonable investor consider important; significant alters total mix of info; market reaction expected.
Non-public test
Chưa được disseminated broad market. Briefing cho sell-side analyst riêng = không "public".
Mosaic theory
Combine public + non-material non-public OK. Cấm combine với material non-public.
II(B) Market Manipulation
Required
Không engage in practices distort prices hoặc artificially inflate volume nhằm mislead.
Information-based
False rumor, "pump & dump", spreading misleading research.
Transaction-based
Wash trade, painting tape, marking close, layering, spoofing.
Legal exception
Tax-loss harvesting, cross-trade settlement OK, market-making, basis arb — miễn không deceive.

Std III · Duties to Clients (1/3)

III(A) Loyalty, Prudence & Care.

Identifying actual investment client là bước đầu tiên — quyền lợi của client beneficial owner là priority, ngay cả khi conflict với người trực tiếp engage member.

  1. Identify the client Pension manager: client = beneficiaries (not plan sponsor). Trust: client = beneficiaries (not trustee). Mutual fund: client = shareholders. Endowment: client = institution's mission. Khi conflict: act for ultimate beneficiary.
  2. Best execution Seek best price + execution quality cho client orders. Soft dollar use OK nếu research benefit client; cấm dùng cho firm overhead. Brokerage chọn dựa trên best execution, không favoritism.
  3. Proxy voting Vote proxy case-by-case based on best interest beneficial owner. Disclose proxy voting policy. Nếu conflict (vd voting for parent company) phải resolve theo policy hoặc abstain.
  4. Recommended Written IPS cho mỗi client. Documented brokerage rationale. Annual review of soft dollar usage. Disclose conflicts trong proxy decision.

01.03 — Guidance 08 / 32
Std III · Duties to Clients (2/3)

III(B) Fair Dealing · III(C) Suitability.

III(B) Fair Dealing
Required
Deal fairly & objectively all clients khi disseminate investment recommendations, take action, change recommendation. "Fairly" ≠ "equally" — tiered service OK; cấm timing advantage trên material info.
IPO allocation
Hot issue / oversubscribed IPO phải allocated pro-rata hoặc theo policy đã disclose. Cấm favorism cho key client. Member bị cấm participate (front-running customer).
Recommended
Limit number of personnel knew before broad release. Establish dissemination protocols. Document allocation procedures.
III(C) Suitability
Required (advisory)
(1) Inquire client experience, risk/return objective, financial constraints; document trong written IPS. (2) Đánh giá suitability ở portfolio context. (3) Reassess client & portfolio định kỳ (≥ annually).
Required (manager fund)
Ensure investment consistent với stated objectives của fund (stated mandate trong prospectus).
Unsolicited request
Client tự đặt order outside IPS — phải warn về unsuitability + có thể require client confirm acknowledgement viết.

01.03 — Guidance 09 / 32
Std III · Duties to Clients (3/3)

III(D) Performance · III(E) Confidentiality.

III(D) Performance Presentation
Required
Communications về investment performance phải fair, accurate, complete. Không cherry-pick portfolios; không hide closed accounts; disclose all material conditions.
GIPS
Compliance với GIPS Standards là cách tốt nhất tuân III(D). Không bắt buộc nhưng strongly recommended cho asset managers.
Recommended
Include all portfolios in composite construction (no survivorship bias). Disclose model returns vs actual. State whether fees gross/net.
III(E) Preservation of Confidentiality
Required
Keep info về current, former, prospective clients confidential.
Exceptions
(a) info về illegal activity; (b) disclosure required by law; (c) client cho phép; (d) PCP inquiry của CFA Institute.
Common pitfall
"Former client" cũng được protect. Ngay cả khi relationship ended, không discuss client info except dưới exceptions trên.

§ III(E) là Standard mà PCP exception thường được test — member phải cooperate với CFA investigation, refusal trigger sanction riêng.

01.03 — Guidance 10 / 32
Std IV · Duties to Employers (1/2)

IV(A) Loyalty.

Loyalty = act for benefit của employer; không deprive employer of skill advantage; không divulge confidential info; không cause harm. Standard này có nuances quan trọng quanh việc nghỉ việc — phân biệt prepare vs solicit vs take property.

  1. While employed — duty active Không undermine employer (vd dissuade clients từ employer's services). Không divulge proprietary info / trade secrets / client list. Compete chỉ after nghỉ việc.
  2. Preparation cho new business — OK Có thể: register entity, lease office, design website, set up bank account, build personal network từ source riêng (LinkedIn cá nhân OK).
  3. Solicitation — KHÔNG OK Không contact current clients để solicit business trong khi vẫn employed. Sau khi resign: được contact nếu info từ memory, không từ records lấy ra. Tuân non-compete clause nếu có.
  4. Property — KHÔNG take Research models, client lists từ CRM, code repositories built tại employer = employer's IP. Cấm copy/email/USB lấy ra. Memorized info OK.
  5. Whistleblowing exception Member được phép act against employer's interest nếu employer engage in illegal/unethical activity. Encouraged report to authority/CFA Institute.

01.03 — Guidance 11 / 32
Std IV · Duties to Employers (2/2)

IV(B) Additional Comp · IV(C) Supervisor.

IV(B) Additional Compensation
Required
Không accept gift/benefit/compensation từ outside party tạo conflict với employer's interest trừ khi có written consent từ all parties involved (employer + outside party).
Examples
Performance bonus từ client cá nhân (extra ngoài normal arrangement) — phải disclose + written consent. Free training/conference từ vendor — modest OK, lavish phải approve.
Phân biệt I(B)
I(B) Independence = về objectivity của analysis. IV(B) = về compensation tạo conflict với employer's interest. Một fact pattern có thể trigger cả hai.
IV(C) Supervisor Responsibilities
Required
Reasonable effort detect & prevent vi phạm laws/regs/Code/Standards bởi anyone under supervision. Setup adequate compliance system.
Inadequate system
Nếu firm không cung cấp system enforcement đầy đủ: supervisor phải decline supervisor role + advise senior management. Continue trong inadequate system = vi phạm.
Detected violation
Phải investigate immediately, increase supervision, document. Continue assigning to violator chưa investigate xong = additional violation.

01.03 — Guidance 12 / 32
Std V · Investment Analysis (1/2)

V(A) Diligence & Reasonable Basis.

  1. Required — reasonable & adequate basis Recommendations phải có reasonable basis backed by appropriate research/investigation. Sole reliance on company management = insufficient. Phải verify independent sources.
  2. Secondary research / external research Khi rely on third-party research, member phải evaluate quality: assumption reasonable? methodology rigorous? sample timely? Nếu OK → may rely + attribute. Nếu không OK → cannot rely.
  3. Group research Member là part of research team với conclusion tổng thể; member cá nhân không đồng ý với conclusion — vẫn OK nếu: (a) team's reasonable basis adequate, (b) member không represent conclusion là của riêng. Có thể request name removed nếu disagree mạnh.
  4. Quantitative model rely-on Khi rely on model — member phải hiểu inputs, assumptions, limitations. Không "black box" reliance. Phải test model edge cases và validate output reasonable.
§ Recommended Establish written policy for research process, source verification, model validation, and peer review. Document basis trong working file (link với V(C) Records).

Std V · Investment Analysis (2/2)

V(B) Communication · V(C) Records.

V(B) Communication with Clients
Required
Disclose distinguishing factors về investment process. Phân biệt rõ fact vs opinion. Disclose limitations + inherent risks.
Material change
Promptly disclose material changes trong investment process / methodology / key personnel cho clients.
Recommendation format
Distinguish "summary" vs "full detailed analysis". Khi present summary, must offer to provide detailed report on request.
V(C) Record Retention
Required
Develop & maintain records support cho investment analyses, recommendations, action, communication.
Default period
7 năm theo CFA Institute recommendation (trừ khi regulator yêu cầu strict hơn).
Ownership
Records là employer's property. Khi nghỉ: cấm take records. Reconstruct từ memory + public sources nếu cần ở firm mới.
Format
Email, working Excel, draft notes, source documents. Audio recording client call nếu disclose & consent.

01.03 — Guidance 14 / 32
Std VI · Conflicts of Interest (1/2)

VI(A) Disclosure of Conflicts.

Standard VI(A) cure-all chính cho conflicts — disclosure đầy đủ, plain language, prominent. Đây là Standard cho phép "disclose then proceed" — khác với front-running (VI(B)) hay duty breach (III(A)) không cure được bằng disclosure.

  1. What to disclose All matters reasonably impair independence/objectivity hoặc interfere với duties to clients/prospective clients/employer. Beneficial ownership, board seat, family connection, fee paid by issuer, soft dollar relationship, broker affiliation.
  2. How to disclose Prominent — không footnote tiny. Plain language — không legal jargon hidden. Front of report hoặc separate page. Update khi conflict change.
  3. When disclosure đủ? Disclosure cure conflict nếu client có thể make informed decision. Không cure: front-running, MNPI trade, fraud — đó là duty breach, không phải conflict.
  4. Recommended Standard disclosure templates trong all reports. Annual personal disclosure form to compliance. Pre-clearance investment outside firm policy.

01.03 — Guidance 15 / 32
Std VI · Conflicts of Interest (2/2)

VI(B) Priority · VI(C) Referral Fees.

VI(B) Priority of Transactions
Required
Investment transactions cho clients + employers phải priority over personal. Personal transaction sau khi client orders đã executed.
Family accounts
Family accounts mà family là client + treated like other clients (no preferential terms) → OK trade like other clients. Family account beneficial owner là member → treated như personal account → priority rule apply.
Recommended
Pre-clearance personal trades. Blackout period 2-3 days around firm research publication. Mandatory holding period 30+ days. Quarterly disclosure to compliance.
VI(C) Referral Fees
Required
Disclose to employer + clients + prospective clients: any compensation/consideration nhận hoặc trả cho recommendation of products/services.
Disclosure scope
(1) Existence của referral arrangement; (2) Nature của arrangement (referral, sponsorship, soft dollar); (3) Value/% basis của compensation.
Timing
Disclose trước consummation of referral. Không "disclose after the fact". Updated nếu arrangement change.

01.03 — Guidance 16 / 32
Std VII · CFA Member Responsibilities

VII(A) Conduct · VII(B) Reference.

VII(A) Conduct as Members & Candidates
Required
Không engage in conduct compromise reputation/integrity của CFA Institute, CFA designation, hoặc integrity/validity/security của CFA exams.
Exam violations
Cheating, sharing confidential exam content (kể cả với colleague preparing for next year), violating exam rules. Cấm reveal nội dung sau khi sit (NDA).
Online behavior
Posting "exam was on chapter X with focus on Y" trên forum/LinkedIn = vi phạm. Trung thực opinion về difficulty OK; specific content KHÔNG OK.
VII(B) Reference to CFA & Designation
"CFA charterholder"
Right to use sau khi đã (a) pass cả 3 levels, (b) hoàn thành work experience requirement, (c) member CFA Institute, (d) đóng annual dues, (e) sign Professional Conduct Statement annually.
"CFA candidate"
Đã enroll & sit (hoặc registered for upcoming) một level. Không "I'm a CFA candidate" nếu chưa register.
Format
"CFA" không phải tính từ. Đúng: "I am a CFA charterholder"; "I have passed Level 2 of the CFA Program". Sai: "I am a CFA"; "I will be a CFA in 2027".
Misrep examples
"CFA Level 3 candidate, expected charterholder Q1 2027" — vi phạm vì "implies eventual charter". OK ghi current candidacy + plain status.

Recap · big picture

Key takeaways.

α
Required vs Recommended
Required = must (vi phạm Standard). Recommended = should (best practice). Recommended absence không tự động vi phạm; nhưng accumulate ill effect cho future case.
β
Identify the actual client
III(A) — pension manager: client = beneficiaries. Trust: client = beneficiaries. Mutual fund: client = shareholders. Khi conflict, act for ultimate beneficial owner.
γ
Disclosure cure SOME, not all
VI(A) conflicts → disclose then proceed = OK. III(A) duty breach, II(A) MNPI, II(B) manipulation, VI(B) front-running → disclosure không cure. Pick most-direct Standard.
δ
Leaving employer — 3 buckets
IV(A): Prepare = OK (entity, office, network từ source riêng). Solicit client current = NOT OK while employed. Take property (research model, client list từ CRM, code) = NEVER OK.

Practice · Question 01 · Original

Who is the client?

§ Vignette
Tuấn, CFA, manages a defined benefit pension fund for a Vietnamese state-owned enterprise. The plan sponsor (the SOE's CFO) instructs Tuấn to invest a substantial portion of the fund into bonds issued by the SOE itself, arguing this will demonstrate confidence and reduce the SOE's borrowing costs. The bonds carry a yield 100 bps below comparable issuers. Under Standard III(A) — Loyalty, Prudence, and Care, Tuấn's primary duty runs to which party?
  1. AThe plan sponsor (the SOE's CFO), because the sponsor engaged Tuấn and pays his management fee.
  2. BThe plan beneficiaries (current and future retirees), because they are the ultimate beneficial owners of the assets and the actual investment client under III(A).
  3. CThe SOE as a corporate entity, because the pension assets are part of the SOE's corporate balance sheet and the SOE is the legal owner.

01.03 — Guidance 20 / 32
Hint · Question 01

Spot the beneficiary.

§ Keywords Pension fund + plan sponsor instructs + SOE bond yield 100 bps below comparable. Ai là actual investment client?
  1. Hint 1 III(A) "identify the client" — pension fund: client = beneficiaries (current + future retirees), KHÔNG phải plan sponsor hay corporate entity. Đây là rule classic.
  2. Hint 2 Investing tại yield 100 bps below market = financially detrimental to beneficiaries. Hành động cho lợi sponsor (lower borrowing cost) ≠ hành động cho lợi beneficiaries.
  3. Hint 3 Tuấn phải refuse instruction này. Có thể cần escalate, document, hoặc resign nếu không sửa được. "Sponsor pays my fee" không phải defense.

Practice · Question 01 · Tiếng Việt

Dịch đề.

§ Vignette
Tuấn, CFA, quản lý một quỹ pension defined benefit cho 1 doanh nghiệp nhà nước (SOE) Việt Nam. Plan sponsor (CFO của SOE) chỉ đạo Tuấn đầu tư phần lớn quỹ vào trái phiếu do chính SOE phát hành, lập luận rằng việc này sẽ thể hiện confidence và giảm cost vay của SOE. Trái phiếu có yield thấp hơn 100 bps so với các issuers tương đương. Theo Standard III(A) — Loyalty, Prudence, and Care, duty chính của Tuấn dành cho ai?
  1. APlan sponsor (CFO của SOE), vì sponsor là bên engage Tuấn và trả management fee.
  2. BPlan beneficiaries (người về hưu hiện tại và tương lai), vì họ là beneficial owners cuối cùng của tài sản và là actual investment client theo III(A).
  3. CSOE với tư cách entity doanh nghiệp, vì tài sản pension là một phần balance sheet của SOE và SOE là legal owner.

Answer · Question 01

Answer: B — beneficiaries.

✓ Correct — B
Standard III(A) yêu cầu identify actual investment client. Cho pension fund, client = beneficiaries (current + future retirees) — họ là ultimate beneficial owner và là người gánh chịu performance. Tuấn phải refuse instruction từ sponsor — đầu tư yield 100 bps below market sẽ harm beneficiaries. Action: document, escalate, có thể resign nếu không sửa. Một action correctness check: hành động này có serve beneficiaries' best interest không? — Rõ ràng KHÔNG.
✗ Wrong — A
Sai cốt lõi — sponsor engage member và trả fee, nhưng client = beneficiary. "Who pays the fee" ≠ "who is the client" trong fiduciary context. Đây là pitfall classic — engagement contract path khác với fiduciary duty path.
✗ Wrong — C
Sai — pension assets không phải part of SOE's corporate balance sheet (defined benefit pension là segregated trust assets). Even nếu SOE là sponsor entity, ultimate beneficiaries là retirees. Không thể subordinate retiree interest cho corporate financing benefit.
Key takeaway
III(A) identify the client: pension → beneficiaries; trust → beneficiaries; mutual fund → shareholders; endowment → institution's mission. "Who pays the fee" hay "who engaged me" có thể khác với "who is the actual client". Action for beneficial owner, even when conflicts with engagement party.

01.03 — Guidance 23 / 32
Practice · Question 02 · Original

Group research dissent.

§ Vignette
An, CFA, is one of five analysts on a sector research team. After a week of joint analysis, the team issues a "Buy" recommendation on a pharmaceutical stock. An personally believes the team's revenue forecast is overly optimistic and that "Hold" would be more appropriate, but the other four analysts disagree after An raised his concerns. The team's overall analytical process — data sources, model validation, and peer review — is rigorous and well-documented. Under Standard V(A), can An's name remain on the report?
  1. ANo — once An personally disagrees with the recommendation, his name must be removed from the report; otherwise he violates V(A) by misrepresenting his views.
  2. BYes — An may stay on the report because the team's process has a reasonable and adequate basis. He can request his name be removed if he disagrees strongly, but is not required to.
  3. CYes — but only if An attaches a written dissent footnote. Without a footnote, his presence on the report violates V(A).

Hint · Question 02

Spot the test.

§ Keywords Group research + An disagrees + team process rigorous. V(A) cho phép gì khi cá nhân disagree?

  2. Hint 2 Nếu disagree mạnh, member có quyền request name removed — nhưng KHÔNG required.
  3. Hint 3 Footnote dissent (đáp án C) là recommended best practice, không phải required. Recommended-only failure ≠ violation.

Practice · Question 02 · Tiếng Việt

Dịch đề.

§ Vignette
An, CFA, là một trong 5 analysts của 1 sector research team. Sau 1 tuần phân tích chung, team issue "Buy" recommendation trên 1 cổ phiếu dược. An cá nhân tin rằng team's revenue forecast quá optimistic và "Hold" sẽ phù hợp hơn, nhưng 4 analysts khác disagree sau khi An raised concerns. Team's overall analytical process — data sources, model validation, peer review — rigorous và well-documented. Theo Standard V(A), tên của An có được giữ trên report không?
  1. AKhông — một khi An cá nhân disagree với recommendation, tên phải removed khỏi report; nếu không sẽ vi phạm V(A) vì misrepresent views.
  2. BCó — An có thể stay on report vì team process có reasonable & adequate basis. An có thể request tên removed nếu disagree mạnh, nhưng không required.
  3. CCó — nhưng chỉ nếu An attach written dissent footnote. Không footnote, presence trên report vi phạm V(A).

01.03 — Guidance 26 / 32
Answer · Question 02

Answer: B — name may stay.

✓ Correct — B
V(A) test cho group research = team có reasonable & adequate basis. Đề nói rõ team process rigorous + well-documented → basis adequate. An không bắt buộc remove name. An có quyền request remove nếu disagree mạnh — đó là option, không phải duty. CFA Institute Handbook explicit: "Members are not required to decline to be identified với conclusion of group nếu team process adequate basis và conclusion is supported by careful analysis."
✗ Wrong — A
Sai cốt lõi — V(A) không yêu cầu personal agreement với conclusion. Yêu cầu là team's reasonable basis. "Misrepresenting views" áp dụng khi member actively misrepresents their personal opinion là conclusion của riêng họ — không phải khi họ part of consensus team.
✗ Wrong — C
Sai phần "without footnote = violation" — written dissent là recommended best practice, không required. Recommended-only failure không tự động vi phạm Standard. Đây là pitfall #2 (Required vs Recommended confusion).
Key takeaway
V(A) group research test = team's reasonable basis, không phải individual agreement. Member có option request name removed nếu strongly disagree (CFA recommended); không có duty to remove. Không confuse "required" với "recommended" — exam đặt distractor với "must" wording cho recommended procedures.

Practice · Question 03 · Original

Outside bonus.

§ Vignette
Linh, CFA, is a portfolio manager at Hanoi Wealth. Her largest client privately offers Linh a "performance bonus" of 1% of any returns in excess of the benchmark, paid directly from the client's personal funds — separate from Hanoi Wealth's normal management fee. The client asks Linh to keep the arrangement confidential from her employer "to avoid bureaucracy." Under Standard IV(B) — Additional Compensation, what must Linh do?
  1. ADecline the bonus outright — accepting any outside compensation always violates IV(B), regardless of disclosure.
  2. BAccept the bonus quietly — the bonus is paid from the client's personal funds, so it does not involve Hanoi Wealth and IV(B) does not apply.
  3. CObtain written consent from all parties involved — both Hanoi Wealth and the client — before accepting the bonus. Without consent, accepting violates IV(B).

Spot the cure.

§ Keywords Outside bonus + conflict với employer interest + client wants confidential từ employer. IV(B) cho phép gì?
  1. Hint 1 IV(B) không cấm outside compensation — cure được bằng written consent từ all parties (employer + outside party). Phải full disclosure.
  2. Hint 2 "Confidential from employer" = explicit refusal disclosure → fail IV(B) cure condition. Member không được accept arrangement này.
  3. Hint 3 Bonus tạo conflict: Linh có thể skew portfolio toward higher-risk strategies để chase benchmark beat → harm khác clients hoặc skew prudent judgment. Đó là why employer cần know & consent.

Answer · Question 03

Answer: C — written consent.

✓ Correct — C
IV(B) không cấm tuyệt đối outside compensation — cure condition là written consent từ all parties involved (Hanoi Wealth employer + client). Bonus tạo conflict: Linh có incentive chase benchmark beat → có thể skew portfolio risk để max bonus, harm khác clients hoặc abandon prudent judgment. Employer cần know để monitor + consent. Trong trường hợp này, client yêu cầu confidential = explicit fail cure condition → Linh KHÔNG được accept arrangement. Linh phải decline hoặc condition acceptance trên full disclosure.
✗ Wrong — A
Sai phần "always violates" — IV(B) cho phép outside comp nếu có written consent từ all parties. Đây là Standard cure-able by disclosure, khác với front-running hay MNPI trade.
✗ Wrong — B
Sai cốt lõi — bonus dù từ client's personal funds vẫn tạo conflict với employer's interest (Linh có thể skew judgment để max bonus). IV(B) explicitly cover compensation từ "outside party" tạo conflict. "Doesn't involve Hanoi Wealth" = misreading Standard.
Key takeaway
IV(B) cure = written consent từ all parties involved. "All parties" gồm cả employer và outside party. Khi client yêu cầu confidential — không thể obtain employer consent → cannot accept. Note distinction: I(B) Independence = về analysis objectivity; IV(B) = về compensation arrangement với employer. Một fact pattern có thể trigger cả hai.
