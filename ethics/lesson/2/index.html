01.02 — Code & Standards 03 / 28
Two documents · one ethical core

The Code & the Standards.

CFA Institute publish 2 documents song song. Code of Ethics nêu principles ngắn (1 trang). Standards of Professional Conduct mở rộng principles thành rules chi tiết áp dụng được (7 Standards × 22 sub-standards).

Code of Ethics
Length
~6 bullet points · 1 trang
Tone
Aspirational principles — "act with integrity", "place client interests above own"
Audience
Tất cả members, candidates, charterholders. Cũng dùng cho marketing, công bố public
Usage
Frame ý thức chung; cảm giác về tinh thần nghề nghiệp
Standards
Length
7 Standards (I–VII) · 22 sub-standards · ~80 trang Handbook
Tone
Rule-based — "must", "shall not", "should not"
Audience
Members & candidates. Bound by enforcement
Usage
Apply để evaluate hành vi cụ thể; basis cho disciplinary action của PCP

§ Khi vi phạm: thường vi phạm cả Code principle lẫn Standard cụ thể. Nhưng PCP sanction dựa trên Standards (rules), không trên Code (principles). Code = compass; Standards = map.

Code of Ethics · structure

The 6 components of the Code.

Members và candidates cam kết: tất cả 6 commitments này phải đồng thời đúng. Memorize tất cả — exam test thẳng "which is NOT a component of the Code".

  1. 1 · Integrity, competence, diligence, respect, ethics Act with integrity, competence, diligence, respect, in an ethical manner với public, clients, prospective clients, employers, employees, colleagues trong nghề, và other participants in global capital markets.
  2. 2 · Place integrity of profession + client interests above self Đặt integrity of investment profession và client interests trên personal interests. (Integrity of profession trước cả client — vì uy tín nghề chung là điều kiện tồn tại của practice.)
  3. 3 · Reasonable care & independent professional judgment Use reasonable care + exercise independent professional judgment khi conduct investment analysis, make recommendations, take action.
  4. 4 · Practice & encourage others — professional & ethical Bản thân hành xử professional & ethical — và encourage others trong nghề làm theo, để bring credit to profession.
  5. 5 · Promote integrity & viability of global capital markets Promote integrity và viability của global capital markets vì lợi ích ultimate của society.
  6. 6 · Maintain & improve professional competence Maintain & improve professional competence của bản thân và strive to maintain & improve competence của other investment professionals.

Standards · taxonomy

The 7 Standards + sub-standards count.

Standards organized theo group đối tượng duty — public, market, client, employer, profession. Memorize tên 7 Standards + số sub — exam test "Standard X has how many sub-standards".

§ The 7 Standards (memorize)
I Professionalism — 4 subs (A: Knowledge of Law · B: Independence · C: Misrepresentation · D: Misconduct)
II Integrity of Capital Markets — 2 subs (A: MNPI · B: Market Manipulation)
III Duties to Clients — 5 subs (A: Loyalty/Prudence/Care · B: Fair Dealing · C: Suitability · D: Performance Presentation · E: Confidentiality)
IV Duties to Employers — 3 subs (A: Loyalty · B: Additional Compensation · C: Supervisor Responsibilities)
V Investment Analysis & Recommendations — 3 subs (A: Diligence · B: Communication · C: Record Retention)
VI Conflicts of Interest — 3 subs (A: Disclosure · B: Priority · C: Referral Fees)
VII Responsibilities as CFA Member/Candidate — 2 subs (A: Conduct as Members · B: Use of CFA Designation)
Tổng: 4 + 2 + 5 + 3 + 3 + 3 + 2 = 22 sub-standards. Nhớ Standard III là largest (5 subs) — duties to client là core của profession.

Std I & II

Professionalism + Integrity of markets.

Standard I — Professionalism (4)
I(A) Knowledge of Law
Know & comply applicable laws/regulations + Code/Standards. Tuân theo cái strict hơn giữa các overlapping rules.
I(B) Independence & Objectivity
Use reasonable care + judgment để giữ independence. Không accept gift/favor compromise objectivity.
I(C) Misrepresentation
Không knowingly misrepresent investment analysis, recommendations, qualifications, services. Cấm plagiarism.
I(D) Misconduct
Không engage in conduct involving dishonesty, fraud, deceit — hoặc adversely reflect trên professional reputation.
Standard II — Capital Markets (2)
II(A) MNPI
Possess material non-public info → không trade hoặc cause others trade. Mosaic theory: kết hợp public + non-material non-public OK.
II(B) Market Manipulation
Không engage in practices distort prices hoặc artificially inflate trading volume. Cấm wash trade, painting tape, false rumor.
Common
Cả 2 Standards ở Std II protect integrity of capital markets — duty không chỉ với cá nhân client mà với entire market participants.
Public effect
Vi phạm Std II thường có public/regulatory consequence (SEC, CSRC, SBV) bên cạnh CFA disciplinary.

Std III · Duties to Clients (5)

The largest Standard — 5 sub-standards.

Standard III có 5 subs vì duty đến client là core của profession. Mỗi sub cover một góc relationship với client.

  1. III(A) Loyalty, Prudence, and Care Act for benefit của clients trên benefit của employer hoặc self. Reasonable care + prudent judgment. Identify actual investment client (vd với pension manager: client = beneficiaries, không phải plan sponsor).
  2. III(B) Fair Dealing Deal fairly & objectively với all clients khi providing investment analysis, recommendations, taking action. Different service levels OK; different timing trên material info KHÔNG OK.
  3. III(C) Suitability (1) Hiểu client investment experience, risk & return objectives, financial constraints qua written IPS. (2) Đánh giá suitability ở portfolio context, không asset-by-asset isolated. (3) Update IPS định kỳ (≥ annually).
  4. III(D) Performance Presentation Communications về investment performance phải fair, accurate, complete. GIPS compliance là cách tốt nhất tuân thủ III(D). Không cherry-pick, không survivorship bias.
  5. III(E) Preservation of Confidentiality Keep info về current/former/prospective clients confidential. Exceptions: (a) info concerns illegal activity, (b) disclosure required by law, (c) client cho phép, (d) PCP inquiry của CFA Institute.

Std IV & V

Duties to Employers + Investment Analysis.

Standard IV — Employers (3)
IV(A) Loyalty
Act for benefit của employer; không deprive employer of advantage of skills/abilities; không divulge confidential info; không cause harm. Cho phép preparation cho competing business; cấm solicit current client + take property.
IV(B) Additional Compensation
Không accept gift/benefit/compensation từ outside party tạo conflict với interest của employer — trừ khi có written consent từ all parties involved.
IV(C) Supervisor Responsibilities
Make reasonable effort để detect & prevent vi phạm laws/regulations/Code/Standards bởi anyone under supervision. Decline supervisor role nếu compliance system inadequate.
Standard V — Analysis (3)
V(A) Diligence & Reasonable Basis
Exercise diligence, independence, thoroughness. Có reasonable & adequate basis backed by appropriate research/investigation cho recommendations. Sole reliance on management = insufficient.
V(B) Communication with Clients
Disclose distinguishing factors về investment process. Phân biệt fact vs opinion. Disclose limitations & inherent risks.
V(C) Record Retention
Develop & maintain records support cho investment analyses, recommendations, action, communication. Default 7 năm hoặc theo regulator.

Std VI & VII

Conflicts of Interest + CFA responsibilities.

Standard VI — Conflicts (3)
VI(A) Disclosure of Conflicts
Make full & fair disclosure của all matters that could reasonably impair independence/objectivity hoặc interfere với duties. Disclosure phải prominent, plain language.
VI(B) Priority of Transactions
Investment transactions cho clients + employers phải priority over personal transactions. Pre-clearance + blackout + holding period.
VI(C) Referral Fees
Disclose tới employer + clients + prospective clients any compensation/benefit nhận hoặc trả cho recommendation of products/services. Đầy đủ: existence + nature + value/% basis.
Standard VII — CFA (2)
VII(A) Conduct as Members & Candidates
Không engage in conduct compromise reputation/integrity của CFA Institute hoặc CFA designation. Cấm cheat exam, share confidential exam content, vi phạm Code & Standards.
VII(B) Reference to CFA & Designation
Khi reference đến membership / CFA designation / CFA Program participation: không misrepresent về meaning/implications. "CFA" không phải tính từ. Candidate phải đã enroll & sit (hoặc registered for upcoming) mới được "CFA candidate".

§ Memorize critical phrases — VII(B) cấm dùng "CFA" như tính từ. Đúng: "I am a CFA charterholder" hoặc "I have passed Level 2 of the CFA Program". Sai: "I am a CFA" hoặc "I will be a CFA in 2027".

Enforcement · PCP

The Professional Conduct Program.

PCP = arm enforcement của CFA Institute. Nhận complaint, điều tra, recommend sanction nếu vi phạm Code/Standards. Process structured và bảo vệ both reporter và respondent.

  1. 1 · Source of inquiries Trigger từ: self-disclosure (member tự report khi event triggers); written complaint từ third party; media report; regulatory notice; thông tin từ annual Professional Conduct Statement.
  2. 2 · Investigation Designated Officer xem xét. Có thể request thêm thông tin; member phải cooperate (refusal có thể trigger sanction riêng). Quá trình confidential.
  3. 3 · Disciplinary Review Committee (DRC) Nếu Designated Officer concluded vi phạm: case escalate to DRC — panel members & volunteers chuyên trách. DRC issue formal sanction.
  4. 4 · Member's options Member có thể (a) accept sanction proposed; (b) reject & request hearing với DRC. Final sanction issued sau hearing nếu rejected.

Sanctions · escalating severity

Available sanctions.

S1
Cautionary letter
Mildest. Warn member nhưng không formal sanction. Confidential. Dùng cho minor or first-time issue.
S2
Public censure / private censure
Formal censure ghi vào record. Public censure có thể publish trên CFA Institute website.
S3
Suspension of membership / candidacy
Tạm đình chỉ membership / right to use CFA designation / right to take next exam. Periodic — vd 6 tháng đến vài năm.
S4
Revocation
Nặng nhất. Revoke CFA charter / membership / candidacy permanently. Public disclosure. Áp dụng cho vi phạm nghiêm trọng (fraud, MNPI trade, repeated violations).

§ Sanction matter — record vi phạm sẽ ảnh hưởng future regulatory licensing (FINRA, SFC, SBV); employer thường require disclosure of any sanction. "Cautionary letter" có thể trông nhẹ nhưng accumulate qua thời gian sẽ tăng nặng cho lần sau.

01.02 — Code & Standards 13 / 28
Member obligations

Annual Professional Conduct Statement.

Mỗi năm, members & candidates phải nộp Professional Conduct Statement — disclose bất cứ event nào trigger inquiry: lawsuit, regulatory action, criminal complaint, written client complaint, etc. Self-disclosure là required; failing = vi phạm thêm.

§ What triggers disclosure? Member phải disclose: (1) any criminal complaint / arrest / conviction; (2) any civil litigation related to professional conduct; (3) any regulatory inquiry / sanction / suspension; (4) any written complaint from client; (5) any termination / suspension by employer for ethics-related cause; (6) any sanction by other professional organization.
§ Continuing duties Beyond annual — member phải continuously aware of changes in laws/regulations affecting practice. Recommended (not required): 20+ hours CE annually, 2+ in standards/regulations. CFA Institute offer CE platform tracking.

Recap · big picture

Key takeaways.

α
Two documents · one core
Code = principles (compass); Standards = rules (map). Sanction dựa trên Standards. Code 6 components: integrity → put profession + client first → reasonable care → encourage others → promote market integrity → maintain competence.
β
7 Standards · 22 sub-standards
I Profession (4) · II Markets (2) · III Clients (5, largest) · IV Employer (3) · V Analysis (3) · VI Conflicts (3) · VII CFA (2). Memorize cả tên + count.
γ
PCP enforcement
Source: self-disclosure, complaint, media, regulator. Designated Officer → investigate → DRC. Member có quyền accept/reject + hearing. Sanction: cautionary letter → censure → suspension → revocation.
δ
Annual Professional Conduct Statement
Self-disclose triggers: criminal/civil/regulatory action, written complaint, employer termination, sanction by other body. Failing to disclose = additional violation. Continuous duty to track law changes.

Spot the tone.

Answer · Question 01

Answer: C.

✓ Correct — C
Code of Ethics tập trung conduct & principles, KHÔNG nói về investment performance target hoặc specific methods. "Maximize risk-adjusted returns ... quantitative methods" là investment objective/method — không phải ethical principle. Code 6 components: (1) integrity/competence/diligence/respect/ethics; (2) place profession + client above self; (3) reasonable care + independent judgment; (4) practice + encourage others to be ethical; (5) promote market integrity; (6) maintain + improve competence (self + others).
✗ Wrong — A
A = Code component #2 verbatim. Đây là một trong những commitment quan trọng nhất — đặt profession + client trên self.
✗ Wrong — B
B = Code component #6 verbatim. Cả 2 phần "self" và "other professionals" đều là Code wording chính xác.
Key takeaway
Code wording không bao giờ nói về specific investment outcome hoặc method (quantitative, fundamental, value, growth, etc.). Khi đáp án mention "maximize", "outperform", "method X" — almost certainly NOT a Code component. Code = how to behave, không phải how to invest.

§ Vignette
A CFA charterholder receives a written client complaint that has been forwarded to the CFA Institute Designated Officer. During the investigation, the member refuses to respond to the Officer's requests for documentation, citing employer confidentiality. Which statement is most accurate?
  1. ARefusing to respond is permissible because Standard III(E) — Preservation of Confidentiality requires the member to protect client and employer information.
  2. BThe member must cooperate with the PCP investigation; refusing to respond can itself trigger a separate sanction. Confidentiality obligations under III(E) explicitly do not apply to PCP inquiries.
  3. CThe member should ignore the inquiry; only the Disciplinary Review Committee (DRC) can compel a response, and DRC action requires a formal hearing first.

Answer · Question 03

Answer: B — must cooperate.

✓ Correct — B
Standard III(E) Confidentiality có exception explicit cho PCP investigation — member được phép (và required) disclose info đến CFA Institute trong quá trình professional conduct inquiry. Member phải cooperate với Designated Officer. Refusal to cooperate tự nó là vi phạm Code/Standards và có thể trigger sanction riêng, ngoài sanction cho vi phạm gốc (nếu có). Process: Officer → investigate → propose sanction → member accept/reject → DRC hearing nếu rejected.
✗ Wrong — A
Sai phần exception — III(E) explicitly cho phép disclose cho PCP. Đây là exception thứ 4 (3 exceptions formal: illegal activity, required by law, client consent — plus PCP). "Confidentiality protects client" không cho phép member refuse cooperate với CFA Institute investigation.
✗ Wrong — C
Sai process — Designated Officer là first level investigation, không phải DRC. Member phải respond ở Officer level. DRC chỉ involve sau khi Officer concluded có vi phạm và case escalate. Ignore Officer = additional violation.
Key takeaway
III(E) exceptions: (a) illegal activity, (b) disclosure required by law, (c) client consent, (d) PCP inquiry. Member must cooperate với CFA Institute investigation; refusal = independent violation. Process: Designated Officer → investigate → propose sanction → member accept or reject → DRC hearing nếu rejected → final sanction.
