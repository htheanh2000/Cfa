Private Equity · strategies

The 4 PE sub-strategies.

PE không phải monolithic — 4 sub-strategies đặc trưng theo giai đoạn của target company và mức độ leverage.

VC
Venture Capital
Early-stage startups, thường tech/biotech. High risk, high return, majority positions fail. Seed / Series A–C.
GE
Growth Equity
Established companies đã có revenue, cần capital để scale. Ít rủi ro hơn VC, ít leverage hơn LBO.
BO
Buyout / LBO
Mature, cash-generative companies. Significant leverage (50–70% debt). Improve ops, exit IPO/sale.
DS
Distressed
Struggling companies, bankruptcy/turnaround. Debt-to-equity conversion, operational restructuring.

§ Risk-return spectrum — VC (highest vol, power-law returns) → Growth → Buyout (lower vol, leverage-amplified) → Distressed (event-driven). Blackstone, KKR, Apollo dominate buyout; Sequoia, a16z dominate VC.

Structure · GP & LP

PE fund architecture.

General Partner (GP)
Role
Fund manager — sources deals, manages portfolio, exits
Commit
1–5% fund capital ("skin in the game")
Fees
2% management + 20% carried interest
Liability
Unlimited — legally responsible for fund
Limited Partners (LPs)
Role
Capital providers — pensions, endowments, SWFs, family offices
Commit
95–99% fund capital (drawn down via capital calls)
Return
80% of profits (after hurdle) + management fee payer
Liability
Limited to committed capital — không lose quá amount commit

§ Fund life cycle — Investment period (year 1–5): GP deploys capital, làm new deals. Harvest period (year 6–10): exit portfolio, return capital. Extensions 1–2 năm thường được phép nếu cần.

Distribution · waterfall

The waterfall.

Waterfall = thứ tự distribute cash từ exit. 4 tiers tuần tự — money flow xuống tier dưới chỉ sau khi tier trên được đáp ứng.

  1. Tier 1 — Return of capital (LP first) 100% cash đầu tiên trả lại LPs cho đến khi họ recover đầy đủ contributed capital. Không split cho GP ở tier này.
  2. Tier 2 — Preferred return (hurdle, thường 8%) Sau khi LP nhận lại capital, LP nhận thêm preferred return (vd 8% IRR) trên capital đã đầu tư. GP vẫn chưa được carry.
  3. Tier 3 — GP catch-up GP nhận 100% cash flow cho đến khi tỷ lệ split overall đạt 80/20 (LP/GP). Catch-up "bù" cho GP khoản họ bỏ lỡ ở tier 1–2.
  4. Tier 4 — Carried interest 80/20 Mọi cash flow thêm được split 80% LP / 20% GP. Đây là carried interest — performance compensation chính của GP.

Return profile · J-curve & exits

The J-curve & exit routes.

§ J-curve pattern PE fund returns initially NEGATIVE (year 1–3) do management fees + portfolio write-downs + chưa có exits, sau đó BẬT LÊN mạnh (year 4–8) khi exits materialize. Vẽ ra đồ thị giống chữ J.
Exit types
IPO
List on public exchange — highest valuation khi market hot, nhưng volatile
Strategic sale
Bán cho corporate buyer — strategic premium, quickest exit
Secondary sale
Bán cho PE fund khác — "secondary buyout"
Recap
Dividend recapitalization — vay debt mới trả dividend cho LPs mà không exit
Performance metrics
IRR
Internal rate of return — time-weighted annualized return
MOIC
Multiple on Invested Capital = Total distributions / capital invested
DPI
Distributed to Paid-In — realized returns only
TVPI
Total Value to Paid-In = DPI + RVPI (unrealized NAV)

Real Estate · vehicles

Real Estate access modes.

RE có thể invest direct hoặc qua funds/REITs. Mỗi vehicle khác biệt về liquidity, fees và control.

01
Direct ownership
Mua property physical. Income từ rent, appreciation. Full control nhưng illiquid, management burden, concentration risk.
02
Listed REITs
Trade trên exchange — liquid, daily pricing. Must distribute ≥ 90% taxable income → dividend-focused. Correlation với equity cao.
03
Non-listed REITs
Không trade public. Thanh khoản kém hơn nhưng không bị daily market volatility. Redemption theo chu kỳ.
04
Private RE funds
GP/LP structure như PE, 7–10 năm life. Core, value-add, opportunistic strategies. Cao fees, illiquid.

§ Property types — Residential, Office, Retail, Industrial/Logistics, Hotels, Timber/Farmland. Post-COVID: office struggling, industrial & data centers boom.

Valuation · approaches

Valuing real estate.

§ Income approach — Cap rate
Value = NOI / Cap rate
Cap rate = NOI / Property value
NOI (Net Operating Income) = rental income − operating expenses (exclude debt service + taxes + depreciation). Cap rate = yield của property — phản ánh risk & growth kỳ vọng. Thấp = đắt hoặc prime location.
  1. Cost approach — replacement cost Value = land + cost to rebuild (minus depreciation). Hữu ích cho new / special-use properties (school, church). Không phản ánh income potential.
  2. Comparable sales approach Benchmark recent sale prices của similar properties gần đây, cùng area, cùng type. Market-driven — cần data đủ từ comparable transactions.
  3. DCF approach Project future NOI cash flows + terminal value, discount về present. Sophisticated nhất nhưng sensitive với discount rate & growth assumptions.

Infrastructure · basics

Infrastructure investing.

Infrastructure = physical assets cung cấp essential services (transport, utilities, communication). Duration dài, cash flows ổn định và thường inflation-linked.

Greenfield
Stage
Dự án mới — đang build, chưa operational
Risk
Higher — construction risk, permitting, cost overrun
Return
Higher expected return để compensate dev risk
Cash flow
Zero / negative trong construction period
Brownfield
Stage
Existing operational assets — acquisition
Risk
Lower — proven cash flows, known operations
Return
Lower return, bond-like profile
Cash flow
Stable, often regulated hoặc contracted long-term

§ Examples — Roads, toll bridges, airports, ports, water utilities, power grids, telecom towers, pipelines, renewable energy. Pensions và SWFs thích infrastructure vì duration match liabilities + inflation pass-through (regulated tariffs tăng theo CPI).

Practice · Question 01 · Original

Compute carried interest.

§ Vignette
A PE fund has $500 million of LP committed capital. After 8 years, the fund returns a total of $900 million to LPs + GP combined. The waterfall specifies: (1) LPs receive their committed capital back first, (2) LPs receive an 8% preferred return (simple, on committed), (3) full GP catch-up to 80/20, (4) remaining split 80% LP / 20% GP. For simplicity, treat the preferred return as a flat $320M owed to LPs after return of capital. What is the GP's carried interest?
  1. A$16 million.
  2. B$80 million.
  3. C$100 million.
CFA Level I ALT INV · 10.02 — Q1 ORIGINAL
Alt Inv · 10.02 12 / 23
Hint · Question 01

Walk the waterfall.

§ Keywords Total to distribute $900M. Committed $500M. Preferred $320M. Full catch-up to 80/20. Total profit = $900M − $500M = $400M.
  1. Hint 1 Tier 1: return capital → LPs nhận $500M. Còn lại $400M.
  2. Hint 2 Tier 2: preferred return → LPs nhận $320M. Còn lại $80M. LPs cumulative = $820M, GP = $0.
  3. Hint 3 Tier 3 (catch-up): GP nhận 100% cho đến khi tỷ lệ đạt 80/20 trên PROFIT (không phải total). Profit được split target: 80% × $400M = $320M cho LP (đã có), 20% × $400M = $80M cho GP. Catch-up đưa GP lên $80M; LP giữ $320M preferred. Total GP carry = $80M.

Dịch đề.

§ Vignette
Một PE fund có $500 triệu committed capital từ LPs. Sau 8 năm, fund trả tổng $900 triệu cho LPs + GP combined. Waterfall: (1) LPs nhận lại committed capital trước, (2) LPs nhận 8% preferred return, (3) full GP catch-up đến 80/20, (4) phần còn lại split 80% LP / 20% GP. Giả định preferred return = $320M cho LPs. Carried interest của GP là bao nhiêu?
  1. A$16 triệu.
  2. B$80 triệu.
  3. C$100 triệu.
CFA Level I ALT INV · 10.02 — Q1 VIỆT
Alt Inv · 10.02 14 / 23
Answer · Question 01

Answer: B — $80 million.

✓ Correct — B
Profit = $900M − $500M committed = $400M. Full catch-up waterfall nghĩa là: sau khi LP nhận prefs, target split trên toàn bộ profit là 80/20 → GP carry = 20% × $400M = $80M. LP total = $500M capital + $320M pref = $820M; GP = $80M. Check: 820 + 80 = $900M ✓.
✗ Wrong — A
$16M = 20% × $80M (phần còn lại sau prefs). Sai — đây sẽ là answer cho waterfall không có catch-up (American-style). Đề nói full catch-up, nghĩa là GP được "bù" để đạt 80/20 trên full profit.
✗ Wrong — C
$100M = 20% × $500M (committed) — áp sai base. Carry tính trên profit (return above capital), không phải committed capital.
Key takeaway
Full catch-up waterfall: GP carry cuối cùng = 20% × profit, bất kể bao nhiêu preferred LP nhận. No-catch-up (American): GP chỉ nhận 20% phần còn lại sau prefs → carry thấp hơn đáng kể. Đọc kỹ "full catch-up" vs "no catch-up" trong đề.

Practice · Question 02 · Original

Value with cap rate.

§ Vignette
An office building generates annual rental income of $3.2 million, operating expenses of $1.2 million, and annual debt service of $400,000. Comparable buildings in the area trade at a cap rate of 6.25%. Using the income approach, the estimated value of the property is closest to:
  1. A$25.6 million.
  2. B$32.0 million.
  3. C$51.2 million.
CFA Level I ALT INV · 10.02 — Q2 ORIGINAL
Alt Inv · 10.02 16 / 23
Hint · Question 02

Spot the trap.

§ Keywords Rental $3.2M, opex $1.2M, debt service $400K. Cap rate 6.25%. Công thức Value = NOI / Cap rate.
  1. Hint 1 NOI = rental − OPERATING expenses. NOI KHÔNG trừ debt service (financing cost), KHÔNG trừ depreciation, KHÔNG trừ tax.
  2. Hint 2 NOI = $3.2M − $1.2M = $2.0M. Debt service $400K là trap — bị loại khỏi NOI vì là capital structure item, không phải operating.
  3. Hint 3 Value = $2.0M / 0.0625 = $32.0M. Nếu trừ debt service (sai), NOI = $1.6M → $25.6M (option A trap).

Dịch đề.

§ Vignette
Một office building có rental income hàng năm $3.2 triệu, operating expenses $1.2 triệu, và debt service hàng năm $400,000. Các building comparable trong khu vực trade ở cap rate 6.25%. Dùng income approach, giá trị ước tính của property gần nhất với:
  1. A$25.6 triệu.
  2. B$32.0 triệu.
  3. C$51.2 triệu.
CFA Level I ALT INV · 10.02 — Q2 VIỆT
Alt Inv · 10.02 18 / 23
Answer · Question 02

Answer: B — $32.0M.

✓ Correct — B
NOI = Rental − Operating expenses = $3.2M − $1.2M = $2.0M. Value = NOI / Cap rate = $2.0M / 0.0625 = $32.0M. Debt service là financing item (capital structure), KHÔNG ảnh hưởng NOI. Cap rate valuation là unlevered.
✗ Wrong — A
$25.6M = ($2.0M − $0.4M) / 6.25% = $1.6M / 0.0625. Trừ debt service khỏi NOI — SAI. NOI by definition là pre-financing, pre-tax. Property value không nên thay đổi theo cách owner funds it.
✗ Wrong — C
$51.2M = $3.2M / 0.0625 — dùng rental GROSS thay vì NOI. Quên trừ operating expenses. Gross rent capitalization dùng GRM (gross rent multiplier), KHÔNG phải cap rate.
Key takeaway
NOI = Rental income − Operating expenses. Exclude: debt service, depreciation, income taxes. Include: utilities, property tax, insurance, maintenance, property management fees. Cap rate approach cho unlevered value — leverage thay đổi equity return, không thay đổi property value.

VC vs LBO.

§ Vignette
Compared with leveraged buyout (LBO) funds, venture capital (VC) funds most likely:
  1. AUse a higher proportion of debt financing in their portfolio investments.
  2. BInvest in later-stage, mature companies with stable cash flows.
  3. CExhibit a wider dispersion of returns across portfolio companies, with a few large winners driving overall performance.
CFA Level I ALT INV · 10.02 — Q3 ORIGINAL
Alt Inv · 10.02 20 / 23
Hint · Question 03

Spot the keywords.

§ Keywords So sánh VC vs LBO. Chú ý debt financing, stage of target, return dispersion.
  1. Hint 1 VC target startup chưa có cash flow ổn định → không thể service debt → VC dùng ÍT debt, không nhiều. A sai (flipped).
  2. Hint 2 VC invest vào early-stage, KHÔNG phải mature. LBO mới invest vào mature companies để leverage được cash flow. B sai (flipped).
  3. Hint 3 VC returns = power-law distribution: majority fail hoặc return < 1x, 1–2 winners trả 20–100x tạo most of fund return. Wide dispersion = đúng VC. C đúng.

Dịch đề.

§ Vignette
So với leveraged buyout (LBO) funds, venture capital (VC) funds nhiều khả năng nhất sẽ:
  1. ADùng tỷ lệ debt financing cao hơn trong portfolio investments.
  2. BĐầu tư vào later-stage, mature companies với cash flow ổn định.
  3. CCó return dispersion rộng hơn giữa portfolio companies, với một vài big winners chi phối overall performance.
CFA Level I ALT INV · 10.02 — Q3 VIỆT
Alt Inv · 10.02 22 / 23
Answer · Question 03

Answer: C — power-law.

✓ Correct — C
VC return distribution highly skewed: roughly 50–60% of portfolio companies fail (0x–1x), majority còn lại return modestly, 1–2 companies ra "home run" (20x–100x+) và tạo phần lớn fund return. Đây là power-law, đặc trưng VC. LBO dispersion hẹp hơn nhiều (debt discipline + mature targets).
✗ Wrong — A
Ngược lại thực tế. VC target companies chưa có stable cash flow → không service được debt → VC đầu tư chủ yếu bằng equity, minimal leverage. LBO DÙNG NHIỀU debt (50–70%) vì target companies có cash flow support leverage.
✗ Wrong — B
Cũng ngược lại. VC invest vào early-stage (pre-revenue, Series A–C startups). LBO mới invest vào mature, stable-cash-flow companies. B mô tả LBO, không phải VC.
Key takeaway
VC vs LBO: VC = early-stage, equity-funded, power-law returns. LBO = mature, leverage-heavy, tighter dispersion. Key mental model: VC bets on few mega-winners; LBO squeezes returns from operational improvement + leverage on already-profitable businesses.
