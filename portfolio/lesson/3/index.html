Philosophy · why portfolio

Portfolio approach.

Thay vì "chọn cổ phiếu tốt nhất", portfolio approach tập trung vào diversification — kết hợp nhiều asset để tối ưu risk-return ở mức tổng thể.

§ Core principle Với ρ < 1 giữa các asset, portfolio SD < weighted average SD. Nhà đầu tư nhận được risk reduction miễn phí mà không giảm expected return — đây là điểm đột phá của Markowitz (1952).
  1. Individual security picking → fragile Tập trung vào 1-2 stocks → idiosyncratic risk lớn. Một earnings miss có thể wipe out capital.
  2. Portfolio diversification → robust Nhiều assets với low correlation → unsystematic risk bị triệt tiêu, chỉ còn systematic risk (market risk).
  3. Investor's goal là portfolio-level outcome Returns và risk được đo ở cấp portfolio, không phải từng asset. Một stock thua lỗ vẫn OK nếu portfolio vẫn đạt mục tiêu.

Process · overview

The 3-step process.

CFA Institute định nghĩa 3 bước tuần tự: Planning → Execution → Feedback. Loop liên tục, không phải one-shot.

01
Planning
Hiểu client, viết IPS (Investment Policy Statement). Định objectives (risk/return) + constraints (LTLU — Liquidity, Time horizon, Tax, Legal, Unique).
02
Execution
Strategic asset allocation → security selection → trade execution. Đây là nơi IPS được translate thành positions thực tế.
03
Feedback
Monitor performance, rebalance về targets, re-evaluate IPS khi client/market thay đổi. Close the loop.
∞
Continuous loop
3 steps lặp lại liên tục suốt life của portfolio. Không stop sau khi invested — quản lý chủ động.

Step 01 · Planning

The IPS — Investment Policy Statement.

IPS là written document định nghĩa mục tiêu đầu tư và ràng buộc của client. Nó là "governing document" — mọi decision sau đó đều phải consistent với IPS.

Objectives · the "wants"
Risk
Willingness & ability to take risk — risk tolerance
Return
Required return (meet liabilities) vs desired return
Balance
Return objective phải consistent với risk tolerance
Constraints · the "musts" (LTLU+L)
L — Liquidity
Cash needs trong short-run
T — Time horizon
Single vs multi-stage; short vs long
T — Tax
Tax status: taxable, tax-deferred, tax-exempt
L — Legal
Regulatory, fiduciary rules (ERISA, trust law)
U — Unique
ESG preferences, concentrated holdings, others

§ IPS phải được review định kỳ (annual) hoặc khi có material change trong circumstances của client (marriage, retirement, inheritance, major market move).

Step 02 · Execution

From IPS to positions.

Execution gồm 3 sub-steps: asset allocation → security selection → trade execution. Mỗi sub-step thêm giá trị (hoặc risk) vào portfolio.

  1. Sub-step 1 — Strategic Asset Allocation (SAA) Chia capital giữa asset classes (equity, bond, cash, alt). Explain > 90% return variance cross-sectionally. Đây là decision quan trọng nhất.
  2. Sub-step 2 — Security selection Trong mỗi asset class, chọn specific securities. Có thể dùng top-down hoặc bottom-up. Active manager thêm value qua step này.
  3. Sub-step 3 — Trade execution Actual buying/selling. Minimize transaction costs, market impact, implementation shortfall. Thường bị underestimated về tầm quan trọng.
§ Rule of thumb Brinson/Singer/Beebower (1986/1991): Asset allocation explains ~90% of return variability over time. Security selection và timing đóng góp phần nhỏ hơn — nhưng có thể quan trọng cross-sectionally giữa các manager.

Step 03 · Feedback

Monitor, rebalance, re-evaluate.

M
Monitor
Track performance vs benchmark, vs peer, vs IPS targets. Attribution analysis: return từ allocation vs selection vs timing.
R
Rebalance
Market moves → drift khỏi target weights. Bring back to policy. 2 styles: calendar-based (quarterly) hoặc threshold-based (±5% band).
E
Re-evaluate
IPS không đứng yên. Life events, market regime, objectives evolve → update IPS. Trigger: annual review hoặc material change.
↻
Close the loop
Feedback quay về Planning. Process là circular, không linear. Portfolio management = continuous discipline.

§ Rebalancing trade-off — Calendar rebalancing simple nhưng có thể rebalance khi không cần. Threshold rebalancing reactive đến market moves nhưng tốn transaction cost khi biến động cao. Combined approach phổ biến.

Investor types · individual

Individual investors.

Individual = natural person hoặc household. Đặc trưng: taxable, finite lifespan, liability = retirement/consumption, risk tolerance heterogeneous.

Objectives
Risk
Tùy thuộc age, wealth, personality — wide range
Return
Fund retirement + intermediate goals (house, education)
Horizon
Multi-stage: accumulation → retirement → legacy
Constraints — usually binding
Tax
Taxable — after-tax return matters. Tax-loss harvesting, asset location
Liquidity
Emergency funds, lumpy expenses
Legal
Estate planning, beneficiary designations
Unique
Concentrated stock (công ty), ESG preferences

§ Life-cycle investing — Young individuals thường tilt equity (long horizon, human capital như bond); retirees tilt fixed income (capital preservation, income). Glide path adjust weights theo age.

Investor types · institutional

Pensions, endowments, foundations.

DB
Defined Benefit pension
Sponsor (employer) chịu investment risk. Liability = future pension payments (discounted). Focus: liability-driven investing (LDI), duration match.
DC
Defined Contribution pension
Employee chịu investment risk. Employer chỉ contribute. Account balance at retirement = invested + returns. 401(k) US, pension Việt Nam DC-like.
EN
Endowments
Nonprofit (thường university) fund. Goal: support operations in perpetuity. Long horizon, return = spending rate + inflation. Tax-exempt.
FD
Foundations
Charitable giving entity. Private foundations: phải spend ≥ 5%/yr (US IRS). Horizon có thể finite hoặc perpetual. Similar logic như endowment.

§ DB vs DC — Global trend từ DB → DC (sponsor chuyển risk). CFA L1 test kỹ sự khác biệt: ai chịu risk, ai quyết định allocation, cash flow pattern. Endowment/foundation thường long horizon → tolerate volatility cao.

Investor types · institutional

Insurance, SWF, banks, mutual funds.

LI
Life Insurance
Liability predictable (actuarial). Long duration. Focus: bonds, immunization, steady income. Risk: low — policyholders expect claim payments.
PC
P&C Insurance
Liability lumpy, short-duration, cat risk. Portfolio: short-duration bonds, high liquidity. Underwriting cycle affects allocation.
SW
Sovereign Wealth Funds
Quốc gia quản lý external surplus (commodity, FX). Rất long horizon, cross-generation. Ví dụ: Norway NBIM, Singapore Temasek/GIC.
BK
Banks & Mutual Funds
Bank: manage excess reserves, short-term liquidity, regulatory-driven (Basel). Mutual fund: pool retail money, regulated vehicle — follow prospectus.

Pooled vehicles · retail

Mutual funds & ETFs.

Mutual funds
Structure
Open-end (mainstream) · closed-end (fixed shares, trade on exchange) · exchange-traded
Pricing
Open-end: NAV end-of-day. Closed-end: market price ≠ NAV (premium/discount)
Fees
Expense ratio 0.5% – 1.5% · có thể có load (sales charge)
Regulation
Heavy (US: '40 Act) — daily NAV, transparency rules
ETFs (Exchange-Traded Funds)
Structure
Open-end nhưng trade trên exchange intraday như stock
Strategy
Phần lớn passive (track index) — SPY, VOO, QQQ
Fees
Very low (0.03% – 0.2%) — cost-efficient
Tax
In-kind creation/redemption → tax-efficient vs mutual fund

§ Open-end vs closed-end — Open-end fund issue/redeem shares tại NAV mỗi ngày. Closed-end fund có số shares cố định, trade trên exchange giống stock → price có thể diverge khỏi NAV.

Pooled vehicles · sophisticated

Hedge funds, SMAs, PE, VC.

HF
Hedge Funds
Accredited/qualified investors only. Flexible strategies (long/short, leverage, derivatives). Fees "2-and-20": 2% mgmt + 20% performance. Lock-up.
SM
SMAs · Separately Managed Accounts
Client own securities directly (không share pool). Customization + tax optimization. Thường minimum $500k – $1M.
PE
Private Equity
Invest vào private companies, buyouts, growth. Closed-end, 10-year life. Illiquid. J-curve pattern: early negative returns → later positive.
VC
Venture Capital
Subset of PE — early-stage startups. Higher risk, higher upside. Power-law returns (few winners drive fund return).
§ Accredited investor US: net worth > $1M (excl. primary residence) hoặc income > $200k/yr. Purpose: investor có đủ sophistication/wealth để hiểu & chịu được risk của vehicle ít regulated.

Management style

Active vs Passive.

Passive management
Goal
Track a benchmark (S&P 500, MSCI) — minimize tracking error
Assumption
Market efficient (EMH) — khó beat sau phí
Fees
Very low (0.03% – 0.2%) — index funds, ETFs
Turnover
Low — tax efficient
Active management
Goal
Beat benchmark (generate alpha) bằng security selection/timing
Assumption
Market có inefficiencies — skill tạo value
Fees
Higher (0.5% – 2%+) — pay for research, expertise
Evidence
Most active managers underperform benchmark over 10+ yrs (SPIVA)
§ Smart beta — the middle ground Rules-based strategies tilt portfolio về factors đã được academic research xác nhận: value, momentum, quality, low volatility, size. Systematic như passive + factor exposure như active. Fee 0.2% – 0.5%.

Selection style

Top-down vs Bottom-up.

  1. Top-down — macro first Flow: macro outlook → asset class → country/region → sector → individual security. Economist/strategist-driven. Phù hợp tactical asset allocation.
  2. Bottom-up — security first Flow: individual company analysis → aggregate lên sector/portfolio. Analyst-driven, focus fundamentals. Buffett-style value investing.
  3. Combined approach Thực tế đa số firms dùng cả hai: top-down cho asset allocation, bottom-up cho security selection trong từng asset class.

§ Asset allocation importance — Brinson studies cho thấy asset allocation (top-down layer) explain phần lớn return variance. Bottom-up selection quan trọng cross-sectional giữa managers, nhưng một portfolio mà allocation sai thì security picking giỏi cũng không cứu được.

Feedback · tactical

Rebalancing strategies.

Drift xảy ra khi asset classes perform khác nhau → portfolio weights lệch khỏi IPS targets. Rebalancing bring back to policy, enforce "sell high, buy low" discipline.

Calendar-based
Trigger
Cố định: quarterly, annually
Pros
Simple, predictable, easy to implement
Cons
Có thể rebalance khi không cần (drift nhỏ) hoặc chờ lâu trong market stress
Threshold-based (percentage-range)
Trigger
Khi weight lệch ra ngoài band (vd. ±5% quanh target)
Pros
Reactive đến market moves, hiệu quả hơn
Cons
Transaction cost cao trong volatile periods, cần monitoring liên tục
§ Best practice — combined Nhiều pension/endowment dùng calendar + threshold: check hàng tháng, rebalance nếu vượt band. Balance giữa discipline và flexibility.

Key takeaways.

1 · Portfolio approach
Diversification > stock picking. Portfolio-level outcome mới là thứ client quan tâm. ρ < 1 tạo free risk reduction.
2 · 3-step process
Planning (IPS) → Execution (SAA → selection → trade) → Feedback (monitor, rebalance, re-evaluate). Loop liên tục.
3 · Investors
Individuals (taxable, life-cycle). Institutionals: DB vs DC pension, endowment (perpetual), foundation (5% rule), insurance (life vs P&C), SWF, banks, mutual funds.
4 · Vehicles & styles
Mutual fund · ETF · hedge (2-and-20) · SMA · PE/VC. Active vs passive · smart beta · top-down vs bottom-up · rebalancing calendar/threshold.

Identify the investor type.

§ Vignette
A large university manages $8 billion of assets intended to fund scholarships and operating expenses in perpetuity. The entity is tax-exempt, has an indefinite time horizon, and typically spends about 4.5% of its asset base annually. Which investor type best describes this entity?
  1. ADefined benefit pension plan.
  2. BEndowment.
  3. CLife insurance company.

Spot the keywords.

§ Keywords University, perpetuity, tax-exempt, indefinite horizon, 4.5% spending. Purpose: fund scholarships/operations.

CFA Level I PORTFOLIO · 06.03 — Q1 VIỆT
Portfolio · 06.03 21 / 28
Answer · Question 01

Answer: B — Endowment.

✓ Correct — B
Endowment = nonprofit educational fund (university), perpetual horizon, tax-exempt, spending rule (~4-5%/yr) — khớp hoàn hảo với vignette. Return objective thường = spending rate + inflation + fees để preserve real value in perpetuity.
✗ Wrong — A
DB pension có defined liability = future benefit payments cho employees về hưu. Horizon dài nhưng finite (finite number of retirees). Typically tax-deferred chứ không tax-exempt. University không có employee pension liability pattern này.
✗ Wrong — C
Life insurance liability = payout theo actuarial mortality tables. Duration long nhưng finite. Corporate entity thường taxable. Investment focus: immunization, duration match — rất khác endowment's equity-tilted perpetual approach.
Key takeaway
Endowment signatures: education/nonprofit, perpetuity, tax-exempt, spending rate ~4-5%. Foundation tương tự nhưng charitable (không giới hạn giáo dục) và private foundation phải spend ≥ 5% (US IRS rule).

Active vs Passive trade-off.

§ Vignette
An advisor is evaluating two equity options for a cost-sensitive, long-horizon client: (i) an S&P 500 index ETF with a 0.03% expense ratio, and (ii) an actively managed large-cap fund with a 0.85% expense ratio and a 5-year return that is 40 bps below the S&P 500. Which statement best characterizes the trade-off?
  1. AThe active fund is preferable because active management always outperforms passive after fees.
  2. BThe passive ETF is preferable for this client: lower fees, and the active fund has failed to generate alpha net of fees.
  3. CBoth are equivalent because expense ratios are irrelevant to long-term outcomes.
CFA Level I PORTFOLIO · 06.03 — Q2 ORIGINAL
Portfolio · 06.03 23 / 28
Hint · Question 02

Spot the keywords.

§ Keywords Cost-sensitive, long-horizon. ETF 0.03% vs active 0.85%. Active return −40 bps below benchmark.
  1. Hint 1 Active fund net return = gross return − fees. Nếu gross ≈ benchmark, net = −85 bps vs benchmark → chưa kể fact là gross chính nó đã miss. Active không "always" beat — SPIVA studies cho thấy đa số underperform.
  2. Hint 2 Fees compound — 0.82% khác biệt qua 30 năm = giảm cuối kỳ ~22% (rule of thumb). Long horizon khuếch đại fee drag.
  3. Hint 3 "Cost-sensitive" + "long horizon" → đặc trưng passive/ETF. Active chỉ justifiable nếu có persistent skill, mà ở đây evidence đã cho thấy không có.

Dịch đề.

§ Vignette
Một advisor đang đánh giá 2 lựa chọn equity cho client cost-sensitive, long-horizon: (i) S&P 500 index ETF với expense ratio 0.03%, và (ii) actively managed large-cap fund expense ratio 0.85% và 5-year return thấp hơn S&P 500 40 bps. Câu nào đúng nhất về trade-off?
  1. AActive fund tốt hơn vì active management luôn outperform passive sau phí.
  2. BPassive ETF tốt hơn với client này: phí thấp hơn, và active fund đã fail generate alpha net of fees.
  3. CCả hai tương đương vì expense ratio không liên quan đến long-term outcomes.
CFA Level I PORTFOLIO · 06.03 — Q2 VIỆT
Portfolio · 06.03 25 / 28
Answer · Question 02

Answer: B — Passive ETF.

✓ Correct — B
Client profile (cost-sensitive, long-horizon) khớp với passive. Expense gap 82 bps là drag lớn, compound qua thời gian. Active fund đã fail beat benchmark (−40 bps pre-fee, nghĩa là net còn tệ hơn). Không có evidence về skill → không justify higher fee.
✗ Wrong — A
"Active always outperforms" là sai về empirics. SPIVA & Morningstar: phần lớn active managers underperform benchmark sau fees trên 10+ years. Active có thể beat, nhưng không luôn.
✗ Wrong — C
Fees rất liên quan long-term outcomes — fees compound đúng cách như returns. 0.82%/yr khác biệt qua 30 năm = vài chục % terminal wealth khác nhau. "Irrelevant" là sai.
Key takeaway
Active justify được khi: (1) có evidence về persistent skill, (2) market segment có inefficiencies, (3) client chấp nhận tracking error. Cost-sensitive + long-horizon + no-skill-evidence → passive là default choice.

The 3-step ordering.

§ Vignette
According to the CFA Institute's portfolio management process, which of the following sequences of activities is correct?
  1. ASecurity selection → IPS drafting → Rebalancing → Asset allocation.
  2. BIPS drafting → Strategic asset allocation → Security selection → Monitoring and rebalancing.
  3. CRebalancing → Security selection → IPS drafting → Performance measurement.
CFA Level I PORTFOLIO · 06.03 — Q3 ORIGINAL
Portfolio · 06.03 27 / 28
Practice · Question 03 · Hint & Answer

Answer: B — Correct sequence.

§ Keywords & Việt Process: Planning → Execution → Feedback. IPS phải trước asset allocation; asset allocation trước security selection; monitor/rebalance là last.
✓ Correct — B
B đúng thứ tự Planning → Execution → Feedback: (1) IPS drafting (Planning — định objectives/constraints), (2) Strategic asset allocation (Execution sub-1), (3) Security selection (Execution sub-2), (4) Monitor và rebalance (Feedback). Đúng chuẩn CFA textbook.
✗ Wrong — A & C
A đặt security selection TRƯỚC IPS → không thể: không biết client mục tiêu gì mà đã pick securities. C đặt rebalancing đầu tiên → không có portfolio để rebalance khi chưa có IPS. Cả hai violate sequence logic.
Key takeaway
Ghi nhớ thứ tự: Plan (IPS) → Execute (SAA → selection → trade) → Feedback (monitor → rebalance → re-evaluate). IPS luôn đầu tiên — không có IPS, mọi decision sau đều unanchored. Feedback close the loop, không đứng độc lập.
